[D] Ministry of Heavy Industries

42.Where were the possible artefacts of the oldest known Wallacean hominids recently found?
[A] Redang Island, Malaysia
[B] Lakshadweep Island, India
[C] Sulawesi Island, Indonesia
[D] Siargao Island, Philippines
Hide Answer

Correct Answer: C [Sulawesi Island, Indonesia]
Notes:
Scientists recently discovered possible artefacts of the oldest known Wallacean hominids on Sulawesi island, Indonesia. Earlier, Homo erectus in Wallacea were believed to have settled only on Flores and Luzon islands about 1.02 million years ago. They were thought incapable of long-distance sea travel, making the Sulawesi discovery important for migration theories. Archaeologists from Australia and Indonesia found small, chipped tools in Soppeng, South Sulawesi, used for cutting small animals and carving rocks. Wallacea is a region in Eastern Indonesia, including Sulawesi, Lombok, Flores, Timor, and Sumbawa.

[A] Karnataka

[D] Madhya Pradesh
Hide Answer

Notes:

44.Vece Paes, who recently passed away, was associated with which sports?
[A] Boxing
[B] Table Tennis
[C] Hockey
[D] Football
Hide Answer

Correct Answer: C [Hockey]
Notes:
On August 14, 2025, Dr. Vece Paes, a 1972 Munich Olympics bronze medal-winning hockey player, passed away in Kolkata at the age of 80 after prolonged illness. He was suffering from an advanced stage of Parkinson’s disease. Born in 1945 in Goa, he combined careers as a medical practitioner and a hockey player. He made his international debut in 1966 at the Hamburg International Cup and later won a bronze at the 1971 World Cup and 1972 Olympics. He represented clubs like Mohun Bagan and East Bengal, winning multiple Beighton Cups and Calcutta League titles.

[A] Invasive weed
[B] Frog
[C] Fish
[D] Moth
Hide Answer

Correct Answer: D [Moth]
Notes:
Scientists recently recorded the rare nocturnal moth species Antitrisuloides catocalina in Choolannur Peafowl Sanctuary, Palakkad district, Kerala. 47.Golden Dome missile defence system, which was seen in the news, belongs to which country?
[A] Russia
[B] France
[C] United States
[D] Germany
Hide Answer

Correct Answer: C [United States]
Notes:
The US President recently announced a 175 billion dollar Golden Dome missile defence shield for the United States. It is a ground and space-based system designed to protect the country from advanced missile threats. Golden Dome will use a network of satellites to detect, track and intercept incoming missiles, including long-range ones from space or other continents. 50.Sulawesi Island, that was recently seen in news, is part of which country?
[A] Singapore
[B] Japan
[C] Indonesia
[D] Vietnam
Hide Answer

Correct Answer: C [Indonesia]
Notes:
An earthquake of magnitude 5.7 recently struck Sulawesi in Indonesia. Sulawesi, earlier called Celebes, is a large island in Southeast Asia and part of the Indonesian archipelago. It belongs to the Greater Sunda Islands and is the 11th largest island in the world with an area of 180,680.7 square kilometers.
