RJS 2025 prelims result: The Rajasthan High Court has released the RJS 2025 Preliminary Examination result on hcraj.nic.in. The cut-off for the General category is 78. A total of 239 candidates were excluded due to OMR sheet errors. Evaluation was done on 93 questions after 7 were deleted. The result includes roll numbers of provisionally qualified candidates. Marks of non-qualified candidates will be uploaded separately. Eligibility for Mains remains provisional, subject to recruitment rules.

HCRAJ publishes RJS 2025 prelims result PDF, 239 candidates excluded over OMR errors RJS 2025 prelims result: The Rajasthan High Court (HCRAJ), Jodhpur, has released the result of the Rajasthan Judicial Service (RJS) Preliminary Examination 2025. The result is now available on the official website of the court, hcraj.nic.in, in the form of a PDF notification containing the roll numbers of provisionally qualified candidates.The Preliminary Examination was conducted on July 27, 2025, for direct recruitment to the Civil Judge Cadre under Advertisement No. RHC/Exam Cell/RJS/CJC/2025/1287. The roll numbers of candidates shortlisted for the Main Examination have been published along with the category-wise cut-off marks.Category-wise cut-off marks for RJS 2025 PrelimsThe cut-off marks for each category were declared in the official notice. For the General category, the cut-off is 78. The cut-offs for other categories are as follows:CategoryCut-off MarksGeneral78General (Ex-Serviceman)46General (Widow)46Scheduled Caste (SC)64Scheduled Tribe (ST)65Other Backward Class – Non-Creamy Layer (OBC-NCL)74Most Backward Class – Non-Creamy Layer (MBC-NCL)59Economically Weaker Section (EWS)75Persons with Benchmark Disabilities – Locomotor Disability (PwBD - LD)40Persons with Benchmark Disabilities – Autism and Multiple Disabilities (PwBD - Autism & MD)43Persons with Benchmark Disabilities – Deaf and Hard of Hearing (PwBD - D&HH)42Persons with Benchmark Disabilities – Blind and Low Vision (PwBD - B&LV)40The court has clarified that the required number of eligible women candidates have qualified within their respective categories, and therefore, no separate cut-off has been released for women candidates.239 candidates excluded due to OMR errorsA total of 239 candidates were excluded from the evaluation process due to incorrect bubbling of roll numbers on the OMR sheet. The reasons included multiple bubbles filled, wrong method used, bubbles left unfilled, use of whitener, or ink spreading over the bubble.Additionally, 77 candidates were excluded from evaluation due to errors in filling the question paper booklet series.How to check and download the RJS 2025 prelims result PDF at hcraj.nic.inStep 1: Visit the official website of the Rajasthan High Court: hcraj.nic.inStep 2: On the homepage, click on the 'Recruitment' sectionStep 3: Look for the link titled “RJS 2025 Prelims Result” or similar notificationStep 4: Click on the link to open the PDF containing the result and cut-off detailsStep 5: Download the PDF and search for your roll number using the search function (Ctrl + F)Direct link to download the RJS 2025 prelims result PDFEvaluation based on 93 questionsWhile resolving objections raised by candidates, 07 questions were deleted from the Preliminary Examination paper. As a result, the OMR answer sheets were evaluated based on the remaining 93 questions, with the maximum marks considered accordingly.Furthermore, any questions answered with multiple bubbles filled, bubbles erased with whitener, or bubbles darkened using incorrect methods were not evaluated.Provisional qualification and eligibilityThe admission of candidates to the Main Examination is purely provisional and subject to the fulfilment of eligibility conditions as specified in the recruitment rules. The statement of marks for candidates who did not qualify for the Main Examination will be uploaded separately on the official website of HCRAJ.In accordance with Sub-rule (4) of Rule 5 of the Rajasthan Rights of Persons with Disabilities Rules, 2018, the shortfall in PwBD vacancies has been filled by interchanging among five sub-categories of PwBD candidates.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
