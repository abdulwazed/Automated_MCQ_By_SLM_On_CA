Rashmika Mandanna and Vijay Deverakonda have once again set the internet abuzz, this time with their undeniable chemistry during an event they attended in New York City. Viral VideoThe rumoured couple, who have been linked for years but have never officially confirmed a relationship, headed to NYC to attend the India Day Parade. The two, twitched up their outfits before heading to attend the Bharat Beyond Borders discussion.Rashmika Mandanna Celebrates 6 Years Of ‘Dear Comrade’ With Vijay DeverakondaCrackling ChemistryA video from the event that turned up online, saw the two all smiles as they chatted with the audience. Fans were quick to point out their relaxed camaraderie and their crackling chemistry as they took questions from the audience. Vijay was also seen leaning towards his rumoured lady love while posing for photos, adding fuel to long-running rumours about their relationship status.Fans ReactThe sighting has sparked a flurry of speculation online, with fans wondering if the two stars are finally ready to make their relationship official. “Is this the sign that they are going to announce their relationship soon...” one user asked, while another wrote, “Stop teasing us and just announce it already!”Dating BuzzRashmika and Vijay, have reportedly been dating under the radar. Although they have yet to post photos together, fans have noted that the two have been sharing photos from similar locations, thus arriving at the conclusion that the two have been travelling the globe, holidaying together. Neither actor has responded to the wave of speculation around their love life, keeping their personal lives guarded as always. For now, fans continue to play detective, trying to spot the similarities in their posts and read between the lines.
