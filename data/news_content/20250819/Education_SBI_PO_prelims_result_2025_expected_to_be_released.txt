The State Bank of India (SBI) is likely to announce the Probationary Officer (PO) Preliminary Examination 2025 result shortly on its official website, sbi.co.in. Candidates will be required to enter their registration number or roll number along with date of birth or password to access the result. Along with the result, the SBI PO scorecard will also be published, while the cut-off marks are expected to be declared a few days later.The exact date and time for the declaration of results have not yet been shared by the bank. However, as per the official notification, the SBI PO prelims result is expected in August or September 2025.How to check SBI PO prelims result 2025Candidates can download their results by following the steps below:Visit the official website of State Bank of India at sbi.co.inOn the homepage, click on the link for “SBI PO Prelims Result 2025.”A login page will appear. Enter your registration number/roll number and date of birth/password.Click on the ‘Submit’ button to view your result.Verify the details mentioned in the scorecard.Download the result and take a printout for future reference.SBI PO 2025: Stages of examinationThe recruitment process for SBI Probationary Officers will be conducted in three stages:Stage 1: Preliminary examinationStage 2: Mains examinationStage 3: InterviewHow to check SBI PO prelims 2025 cut-off marksThe SBI PO prelims cut-off marks, once released, can be accessed online through the official website using the required login credentials.The preliminary examination was conducted on August 4, 2025 to recruit candidates for 541 probationary officer posts. Applicants scoring marks equal to or above the prescribed cut-off will qualify for the next stage of the selection process. The result will be based on normalisation of scores.
