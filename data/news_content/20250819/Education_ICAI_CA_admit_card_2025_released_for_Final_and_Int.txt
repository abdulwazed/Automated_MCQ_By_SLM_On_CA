ICAI CA admit card 2025 released for Final and Inter exams; download at icai.org The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) has released the ICAI CA admit card 2025 for candidates appearing in the September session of the Final and Intermediate examinations. The admit cards are available for download on the official ICAI website — icai.org.Candidates registered for the CA Final and Inter examinations can now access and download their admit cards by logging into the student portal. The admit card is a mandatory document for entry into the examination centre, and candidates must carry a hard copy to the exam hall.Admit card released for Final and Inter September 2025 sessionThe admit card is available for both the Final and Intermediate courses. As per the official timetable, the Final course examination for Group 1 will be conducted on September 3, 6 and 8, 2025, and Group 2 will be held on September 10, 12 and 14, 2025. For the Intermediate course, Group 1 exams will take place on September 4, 7 and 9, 2025, while Group 2 exams will be conducted on September 11, 13 and 15, 2025.No examination is scheduled on September 5, 2025 (Friday) due to Milad-un-Nabi, a Central Government gazetted holiday.Exam duration and reading time instructionsThe duration of most examination papers is three hours. However, Paper 3 and Paper 4 of the Foundation examination will be held for two hours, and Paper 6 of the Final examination will be of four hours duration. All papers, except Paper 3 and Paper 4 of the Foundation course, will include an advance reading time of 15 minutes from 1:45 PM (IST) to 2:00 PM (IST). There will be no advance reading time for Foundation Papers 3 and 4.ICAI CA admit card 2025: How to downloadCandidates can follow the steps below to download the ICAI CA admit card 2025 for Final and Inter examinations:Step 1: Visit the official website of ICAI at icai.org.Step 2: Click on the ‘Students Login’ section on the homepage.Step 3: Select the ICAI CA admit card 2025 link for Final or Inter examinations.Step 4: Enter the required login credentials and submit.Step 5: View and download the admit card, and take a printout for future reference.Direct link to download ICAI CA admit card 2025 for September Final and Inter examsFurther details available on the official websiteFor more information regarding the admit card, examination schedule, and paper duration, candidates are advised to visit the official website of ICAI at icai.org.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
