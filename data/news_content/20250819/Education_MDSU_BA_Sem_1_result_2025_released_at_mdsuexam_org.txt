Maharshi Dayanand Saraswati University (MDSU), Ajmer has officially declared the Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) Part-I (Semester-I) examination result on August 18, 2025. More than 25,000 students who appeared for the examination were waiting for the announcement. The results have been published online on the university’s official portal: mdsuexam.orgThe B.A. Part-I (Semester-I) examination was held in the December 2024 session between December 11, 2024, and March 26, 2025. Candidates can now log in using their student credentials to access and download their scorecards.How to check MDSU BA Sem 1 result 2025Here is a step-by-step guide to check the MDSU BA Sem 1 result 2025 online:Visit the official website of Maharshi Dayanand Saraswati University: mdsuexam.orgOn the homepage, locate the panel for NEP (National Education Policy) classes and click on it.Navigate to the student panel section.Log in using the necessary credentials such as username and password.The result will be displayed on the screen.Download the scorecard and take a printout for future reference.Direct link to check the MDSU BA Sem 1 result 2025 is available here. Students are advised to carefully verify the details mentioned on their scorecards. In case of any discrepancy, they should immediately contact the university examination authorities for rectification.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
