(Picture Courtesy: Facebook) Malayalam superstar Fahadh Faasil surprised fans with a nostalgic moment during the promotions of his upcoming film 'Odum Kuthira Chaadum Kuthira' (OKCK). At a college event in Ernakulam, the actor recreated his now-iconic dance steps from last year’s blockbuster Aavesham, sending the crowd into a frenzy.A viral dance that lives onThe event saw Fahadh join his co-stars Kalyani Priyadarshan, Revathi Pillai, Vinay Forrt, and director Althaf Salim on stage.Odum Kuthira Chaadum Kuthira - Official TrailerWhen the beats of 'Aavesham’s chartbuster 'Odimaga' started playing, fans cheered as Fahadh slipped back into his viral moves, proving yet again why the performance had become such a cultural sensation.The students were seen chanting the 'Pushpa' actor's name and capturing the moment on their phones. Interestingly, Fahadh Faasil is also seen calling Kalyani Priyadarshan and others to join the dance.The video is going viral on social media for all the righ reasons. One Tweet read, "Rangan Chetta Neegala (Is this you Rangan chettan?)." Another one commented, "Fahadh Faasil dance is fire."PollAre you excited about the Onam release of 'Odum Kuthira Chaadum Kuthira'?Absolutely!Not really.Check out the video here.Promotions filled with fun and energyThe promotional event wasn’t just about dance. Fahadh and the team interacted with the students, sharing anecdotes from the shoot of'Odum kuthira Chadum Kuthira'The recently released trailer of OKCK offers a glimpse of Fahadh in a romantic role and the story unfolds around a wedding, with his character Aby caught between Kalyani Priyadarshan and Revathi Pillai’s characters. Vinay Forrt’s presence adds a comic flavor, while director Althaf Salim promises a blend of emotions wrapped in lighthearted fun.Onam release around the corner'Odum Kuthira Chaadum Kuthira' is set for a festive release on August 29, coinciding with Onam celebrations. The music is composed by Justin Varghese and has vibrant visuals shot across Kerala, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu.
