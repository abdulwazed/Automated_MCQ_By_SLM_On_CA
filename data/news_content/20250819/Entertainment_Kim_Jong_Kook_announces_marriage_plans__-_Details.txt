Kim Jong Kook, a renowned Korean singer who is also known for his variety skills, is officially tying the knot!In a recent episode of a variety show, the star made the big revelation, shocking everyone on set. The news comes as a surprise to many as the hunk has managed to keep his love life rather private.Kim Jong Kook announces marriageIn a recent episode of a popular Korean variety show titled 'Running Man,' Jong Kook made the shocking revelation on August 18, which the cast members first thought was a part of a prank segment. The singer shared that he will soon be tying the knot and getting married, which managed to surprise everyone.Along with the regular cast members, Jong Kook was also joined by renowned actors Jang Dong Yoon and Kim Ah Young for that day's segment, which was being filmed in Pyeongtaek, Gyeonggi Province.As per Allkpop, he also shared the news with his fans and netizens through a post on a popular fan forum.He shared a heartfelt message that read, “I always thought the day would come when I’d have to write something like this, and I had prepared myself for it. But now that I’m actually writing, I’m more nervous and anxious than I ever imagined. I’m getting married."He also talked about how even though it may be a little late for him, it is still something that he is looking forward to, and also asked his fans for their support and love in this next chapter of his life. He also jokes about how, instead of sharing a new album for his 30th anniversary, he ended up getting a partner instead.The post read, "I know I’ve dropped little hints here and there, but I’m sure it still feels sudden to many of you. This year marks my 30th debut anniversary, and instead of making the album I wanted, I’ve created my other half. Please congratulate and support me."Who is the mysterious woman? While Jong Kook shared that he is about to tie the knot, he did not provide many details about who his soon-to-be wife is. Netizens and fans, since the announcement, have been trying to piece together clues that could clarify who she may be.Recently, Sports Chosun shared a report about who she may be. In the report, it was mentioned that the idol may be getting married to a Korean American businesswoman from Los Angeles.Rumours of him dating an American woman have been going on since 2018, and while nothing has been confirmed by the idol himself, fans have been wondering about whether the rumours were true after all or not."Get the latest updates on Times of India, including reviews of the movie Coolie and War 2."About the AuthorTOI Entertainment DeskThe TOI Entertainment Desk is a dynamic and dedicated team of journalists, working tirelessly to bring the pulse of the entertainment world straight to the readers of The Times of India.
