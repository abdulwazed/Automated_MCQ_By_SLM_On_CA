"Tomorrow is not promised" is what YouTubers NinaUnrated and Patrick Blackwood have to say after they met an unexpected and terrifying incident while enjoying their food. A chilling video of the two food reviewers is doing rounds on social media, where they can be seen sitting in a restaurant, Piada Italian Street Food, in Tyler, Texas. Suddenly, a car ploughed into the restaurant, hitting the pair, who sustained minor injuries. The incident took place when an SUV suddenly crashed through the glass wall, showering the pair, who were recording a food review video, with debris.Blackwood was the one impacted the most as he was sitting next to the window. He suffered several cuts due to the shattered glass. "I'm beyond grateful to be alive after an SUV crashed through the glass wall at Cuvee Culinary Creations in Houston, TX, shattering everything while Patrick Blackwood and I recorded an eating show," NinaUnrated wrote on Instagram."It hit us directly me on his left, him on my right as I bit into a delicious salmon slider. Out of nowhere, but we survived," she added. Blackwood, in a post on TikTok, said, "Grateful to see another day, walking away from the small stuff with a big purpose!"
