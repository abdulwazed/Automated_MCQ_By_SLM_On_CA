NEET PG 2025 result link to be activated at NBE and Natboard websites NEET PG 2025 result: The National Board of Examinations in Medical Sciences (NBEMS) is expected to announce the NEET PG 2025 result shortly. Candidates who appeared for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test for Postgraduate (NEET PG) 2025 will be able to check and download their scorecards from the official website, nbe.edu.in, once the result link is activated.This year, more than 2.42 lakh candidates appeared for the NEET PG 2025 exam, which was conducted in a single shift in computer-based mode across 301 cities and 1,052 exam centres. The exam was held for admission to MD, MS, and PG Diploma courses in medical institutions across India.NEET PG 2025 result link to be activated at NBE and Natboard websitesAs per official updates, the NEET PG 2025 result and scorecard will be hosted on the NBEMS websites — nbe.edu.in and natboard.edu.in. Candidates will be required to log in using their credentials to download the scorecard, which will display the marks secured, rank obtained, and other personal and examination details.In 2024, NBEMS had declared the NEET PG result within 12 days of conducting the examination. However, this year, more than two weeks have passed since the exam was held. The board has not yet confirmed whether it will release the answer key or directly announce the final results.Cut-offs likely to rise as exam seen as easierAccording to candidates, the NEET PG 2025 exam was of easy to moderate difficulty, which may lead to an increase in the qualifying cut-offs. Vaibhav Sharma, an MBBS student from Pt JNMMC, remarked that it felt like the easiest NEET PG in years, suggesting a possibility of rank inflation.NBEMS will publish cut-offs for all categories along with the result. Last year, the NEET PG results were declared on August 23.Supreme Court directives on NEET PG counsellingFollowing several petitions, the Supreme Court issued ten directives to ensure transparency in the NEET PG counselling process. These include a synchronised counselling schedule for MCC All India Quota and state counselling, Aadhaar-based seat tracking, publication of answer keys and raw scores, and penalties for seat blocking.Private and deemed universities are required to disclose their fee structures. A centralised fee regulation mechanism will be supervised by the National Medical Commission (NMC), and state authorities will be held accountable for any rule violations. NBEMS has been directed to establish an independent oversight mechanism under the NMC to audit the counselling process annually.PG seats available for NEET PG 2025 admissionsAs per the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, there are 74,306 postgraduate seats available for medical admissions in India this year. Between 2014 and 2025, PG seats have increased by 138.3%, while the number of medical colleges has grown by 101.5%, from 387 to 780.How to check NEET PG 2025 result at NBE.edu.inStep 1: Visit the official website: nbe.edu.inStep 2: Click on the “NEET PG 2025” section on the homepageStep 3: Select the link for “NEET PG 2025 result”Step 4: Enter your login credentials (User ID and Password)Step 5: Download the scorecard and save it for future referenceNEET PG 2025 result details and marking schemeThe NEET PG 2025 paper carries a total of 800 marks. Candidates are awarded four marks for every correct answer, and one mark is deducted for each incorrect response.Candidates are advised to regularly check the official websites — nbe.edu.in and natboard.edu.in — for the latest updates on result release, cut-offs, and the counselling process.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
