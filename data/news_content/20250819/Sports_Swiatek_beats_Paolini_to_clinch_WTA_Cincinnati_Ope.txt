Swiatek beats Paolini to clinch WTA Cincinnati Open title
Iga Swiatek secured her first Cincinnati Open title, defeating Jasmine Paolini in straight sets. The victory, Swiatek's 11th WTA 1000 title, brings her closer to Serena Williams' record and boosts her confidence heading into the US Open. This win also puts her within striking distance of the WTA number one ranking currently held by Aryna Sabalenka.

Iga Swiatek Iga Swiatek filled in a piece of her career tennis puzzle on Monday as she won the ATP-WTA Cincinnati Open for the first time with a 7-5, 6-4 defeat of Jasmine Paolini. The final lasting for just under two hours featured 16 break-point chances, with the Polish winner converting on six of 10 while Paolini struggled to make the most of her break opportunities.The six-time Grand Slam singles champion's 11th career win at the WTA 1000 level has left her within striking distance of the all-time record of 13 titles held by Serena Williams.
