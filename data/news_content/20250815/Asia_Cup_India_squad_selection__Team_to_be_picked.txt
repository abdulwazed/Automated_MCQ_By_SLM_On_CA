Suryakumar Yadav (Image credit: X) MUMBAI: The Indian team for the Asia Cup, which will begin from September 9 in the UAE, will be picked on August 19 in Mumbai, TOI has learnt. "Yes, the team for the Asia Cup will be picked on Aug 19 in Mumbai. The selection committee meeting will be followed by a press conference by chief selector, former India pacer Ajit Agarkar," a source in the BCCI said on Thursday.
