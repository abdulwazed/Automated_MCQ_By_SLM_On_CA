India's 79th Independence Day quiz 2025: Try this MCQ challenge on the freedom movement As India approaches the 79th anniversary of its independence on 15 August 2025, it is a fitting time to reflect on the remarkable journey that led to the nation's freedom. From decades of resistance to the eventual transfer of power, India’s struggle for independence was marked by extraordinary resilience, unity, and courage.This quiz invites students to explore the critical moments, influential figures, and enduring symbols associated with India's freedom movement. Whether you're brushing up on history or testing what you already know, these questions aim to deepen your understanding of India’s past and the spirit of its people.Independence Day quiz: How well do you know India’s history?1.
