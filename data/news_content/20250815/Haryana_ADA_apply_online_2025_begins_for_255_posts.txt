Haryana ADA apply online 2025 begins at HPSC for 255 posts, check eligibility and how to apply HPSC ADA recruitment 2025: The Haryana Public Service Commission (HPSC) has officially commenced the online application process for the recruitment of Assistant District Attorney (ADA) posts in the Prosecution Department of Haryana. A total of 255 vacancies have been announced under Advertisement No. 18/2025.As per the official notification released on August 8, 2025, the application window opened on August 13, 2025, and will remain active until September 2, 2025, up to 5:00 PM. Eligible candidates can apply through the official website of the commission at hpsc.gov.in.Application and vacancy details for Haryana ADA recruitment 2025The recruitment drive is for 255 ADA posts in the Prosecution Department of Haryana. Candidates must possess a Bachelor’s degree in Law (LLB) from a recognised university and be enrolled as an Advocate with the Bar Council. Additionally, proficiency in Hindi or Sanskrit up to the Matriculation level is mandatory.Below is the category-wise distribution of vacancies:CategoryNo. of VacanciesGeneral (UR)134Other Scheduled Castes (OSC)26Denotified/Scheduled Castes (DSC)26Backward Class A (BCA)28Backward Class B (BCB)15Economically Weaker Section (EWS)26Total255Eligibility criteria for Haryana ADA 2025 recruitmentTo be eligible for the ADA post, a candidate must:• Hold a Bachelor’s degree in Law (LLB) from a recognised university• Be enrolled as an Advocate with the Bar Council• Have studied Hindi or Sanskrit up to the Matriculation level• Be aged between 21 and 42 years as of September 2, 2025 (age relaxation applies as per Haryana government rules)Direct link to apply online for Haryana ADA recruitment 2025Haryana ADA application fee detailsCandidates must pay the application fee online using Net Banking, Debit Card, or Credit Card before 5:00 PM on September 2, 2025. The fee is non-refundable and varies by category:CategoryApplication Fee (INR)General / Other State1000SC / BC-A / BC-B / EWS / ESM / Women250PwBD (Persons with Benchmark Disability)NilDocuments required for Haryana ADA applicationApplicants must upload scanned copies of the following documents:• LLB degree certificate• Bar Council enrolment certificate• Recent passport-size photograph and signature• Valid identity proof (Aadhaar, passport, etc.)• Caste/category certificate (if applicable)• Any other documents specified in the official notificationHow to apply online for Haryana ADA recruitment 2025Candidates can follow the steps below to apply:Step 1: Visit the official HPSC website: hpsc.gov.inStep 2: Register using Aadhaar number or mobile credentials (ensure Aadhaar data is updated)Step 3: Log in to access the “Apply Online” section and locate Advertisement No. 18/2025Step 4: Fill in personal, educational, and professional details accurately, including Bar Council informationStep 5: Upload required documents, pay the application fee, and submit the form. Download and print the confirmation page for recordsSelection process for Haryana ADA 2025 recruitmentThe selection process may consist of the following stages:• Screening test (if required)• Subject knowledge test• Interview / Viva-voce• Document verificationOfficial website for applicationThe application process must be completed online via the official portal at hpsc.gov.in.
