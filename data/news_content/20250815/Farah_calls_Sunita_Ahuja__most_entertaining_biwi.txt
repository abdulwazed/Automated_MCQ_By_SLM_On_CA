Govinda’s wife Sunita Ahuja, known for her bold and lively nature, has made her debut on YouTube with her very first vlog released yesterday. Following the teaser, many celebrities, including Farah Khan and Suniel Shetty, showed their support. Farah, who shares popular cooking videos with her chef Dilip, praised Sunita as the most entertaining wife in Bollywood, while Suniel appreciated her for staying true to herself without any apologies.Farah Khan’s Daughter Diva Looks Like Mini Alia Bhatt In Viral Portugal PicFarah Khan and Suniel Shetty show their supportFarah eagerly welcomed Sunita to the YouTube platform by sharing the teaser of her debut vlog. Praising the content, Khan called her the “Most entertaining biwi!! Now on YouTube," and encouraged her followers to watch it. At the same time, Suniel Shetty took to Instagram stories to cheer Sunita on, writing, “YouTube India, brace yourself! Sunita Ahuja- only you can be unapologetically YOU- raw, honest, and downright entertaining. And now the world’s about to get hooked. This is gonna be less watch & chill and more laugh till I spill!”.Teaser highlights Sunita’s bold entranceOn Instagram yesterday, Sunita gave a sneak peek of her first YouTube vlog, captioning it, “Biwi No 1 aab aa chuki hai youtube channel par please jake dekho mere youtube video link in bio like karo share karo subscribe kardoo and comment me batao kaisa laga aapko." The teaser features Sunita making a grand entrance with the song ‘Biwi No. 1’ playing. She introduces herself to viewers, saying, “Hi guys, this is Sunita. Aap dekh rahe ho mujhe YouTube channel par. Sabne paise kamaya, aab meri baari hai. Aab main kamaungi. Chaapungi." The video closes with her proudly displaying her collection of gold jewelry.Sunita addresses divorce rumours in first vlogThe teaser gives a glimpse of Sunita’s first vlog, where she plans to address the rumours about her divorce from Govinda. She says, “Yeh ek saal mere liye bahut difficult raha hai. Pata nahi kitne logo ne kya kya bakwaas kiya hai mere baare mein." In the video, she is also shown purchasing alcohol, adding, “Yeh mat sochna yeh mere liye le rahe hu main.
