UPSC CSE main 2025 admit card out at upsc.gov.in for August 22 exam UPSC CSE main 2025 admit card: The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released the admit card for the UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE) Main 2025. Candidates registered for the examination can now download their hall tickets from the official website, upsc.gov.in. The exam is scheduled to be held on August 22, and candidates must carry the admit card along with a valid photo ID to gain entry to the exam centre.The UPSC CSE Main 2025 admit card will remain available for download until August 31. However, candidates are advised to obtain their admit cards well before the exam date to avoid any last-minute difficulties. It is important to verify all details on the admit card to ensure accuracy and to report any discrepancies to the UPSC helpline promptly.Essential documents and instructions for the exam dayCandidates appearing for the UPSC CSE Main 2025 must carry a printed copy of their admit card as well as an original photo identification card. Accepted forms of photo ID include Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, or Voter ID. Both documents must match exactly, as discrepancies can result in denial of entry to the examination hall.The commission prohibits mobile phones, smartwatches, and other electronic gadgets inside the exam venue. Candidates are also advised against carrying bags or valuables, as there will be no storage facilities at the exam centre. It is vital to follow all instructions printed on the admit card, including the reporting time and permitted stationery, to avoid disqualification.Steps to download the UPSC CSE main 2025 admit card at upsc.gov.inStep 1: Visit the official UPSC website at upsc.gov.in.Step 2: Navigate to the 'Admit Card' or 'Download Hall Ticket' section on the homepage.Step 3: Enter your registration ID or roll number along with your date of birth in the specified format.Step 4: Submit the details to access your admit card.Step 5: Download and print at least two copies of the admit card – one for the exam day and a backup copy.Direct link to download the UPSC CSE main 2025 admit cardVerifying details and resolving discrepanciesCandidates should carefully check all personal information on their admit card, including name, photograph, and exam centre address. Any errors must be reported immediately to the UPSC helpdesk via email at helpdesk@upsc.gov.in or by calling the official helpline numbers. Early reporting helps ensure corrections are made before the exam day, preventing complications at the centre.Exam structure and eligibility rulesThe UPSC CSE Main consists of nine written papers followed by a personality test. Seven papers count towards the final merit list, while two language papers are qualifying in nature. Candidates must write the language papers in the script corresponding to their chosen language.Eligibility to appear for the mains is limited to candidates who have qualified in the Preliminary exam. Candidates previously appointed to the IAS or IFS are not eligible to appear again, while IPS appointees may reappear but cannot opt for the IPS cadre again. Carrying a valid degree certificate or provisional mark sheet along with the admit card is mandatory.Exam day tips and covid-19 protocolsCandidates are advised to plan their route to the exam centre well in advance and arrive early to avoid last-minute stress. Printing multiple copies of the admit card and safely storing them is recommended. Depending on the prevailing health guidelines, candidates may need to follow Covid-19 safety measures, including wearing masks and carrying hand sanitiser, as specified on the admit card.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
