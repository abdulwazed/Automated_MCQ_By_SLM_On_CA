CSBC Bihar Police Constable answer key 2025: The Central Selection Board of Constables (CSBC) is set to release the provisional answer key for the Bihar Police Constable Recruitment Examination 2025 in the coming days. Candidates will be able to download it from the official website — csbc.bihar.gov.in — once it is published.The written examination, conducted on July 16, 20, 23, 27, 30, and August 3, marked the first stage of the selection process. This stage comprised 100 multiple-choice questions (MCQs), each carrying one mark, to be completed within a two-hour duration.The recruitment drive aims to fill 19,838 vacancies for constable posts across Bihar. Following the release of the answer key, candidates qualifying in the written examination will progress to the Physical Efficiency Test (PET). For the PET round, the CSBC will shortlist candidates at a ratio of five times the number of available vacancies, strictly on the basis of merit.CSBC Bihar Police Constable answer key 2025: Steps to downloadCandidates can follow the steps mentioned here to download the CSBC Bihar Police Constable answer key 2025 once it is released:Visit the official CSBC website at csbc.bihar.gov.in.On the homepage, click the link for the Bihar Police Constable answer key 2025.Enter your registered login credentials.Submit the details and download the answer key for future use.Candidates are advised to stay tuned to the official website to get the complete details of the CSBC Bihar Constable police answer key 2025.Ready to navigate global policies?
