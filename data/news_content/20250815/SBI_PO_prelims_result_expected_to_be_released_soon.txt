SBI PO prelims result 2025 expected soon SBI PO prelims result 2025 expected to be released soon: The State Bank of India (SBI) is expected to release the results of the Probationary Officer (PO) prelims exam 2025 soon on the official website. The nationwide Prelims were held on August 2, 4, and 5, attracting thousands of aspirants across multiple test centres.While the exact date for result declaration has not been confirmed, SBI typically releases results within weeks of the exam. Candidates have been urged to keep a close watch on the official SBI Careers portal — sbi.co.in — to avoid missing updates.Only those who clear the Prelims will move forward to the Main Examination, the next crucial stage in the high-stakes SBI PO recruitment process.SBI PO prelims results 2025: Steps to download scorecardsCandidates can follow the steps mentioned here to download scorecards from the official website once it is released:Visit the SBI Careers portal at sbi.co.in.Navigate to the Recruitment Results section.Locate “Probationary Officers (Advertisement No: CRPD/PO/2024-25/22)” under the Prelims results tab.Click on the link as provided here.Enter roll number/registration number and date of birth accurately.Click submit to view the result. The result will appear on the screen.Download and print the result for future reference, especially for the Mains exam registration.SBI PO prelims scorecard 2025: Details includedThe scorecard will include:Candidate details: Name, Roll/Registration Number, Date of Birth, Category.Exam details: Name, date, and shift/session of the exam.Section-wise scores: English, Quantitative Aptitude, and Reasoning Ability.Overall performance: Total marks obtained and maximum marks.Qualifying status: Confirmation of Main Exam eligibility.Cut-off marks: Category-wise section and overall cut-offs.Next stage: Main exam and beyondPrelims marks are qualifying in nature and do not contribute to the final merit list. Candidates who clear this stage will proceed to the Main Examination, which carries greater weight in the selection process. The Main Exam will be followed by the Group Exercise and Interview rounds, which collectively determine the final list of successful candidates for the prestigious SBI PO posts.Candidates are advised to stay tuned to the official website to get the latest updates of the SBI PO recruitment process.Ready to navigate global policies?
