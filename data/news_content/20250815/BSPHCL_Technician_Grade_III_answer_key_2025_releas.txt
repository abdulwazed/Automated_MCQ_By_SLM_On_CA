BSPHCL Technician Grade III answer key 2025 released at bsphcl.co.in BSPHCL Technician Grade III answer key 2025: The Bihar State Power Holding Company Limited (BSPHCL) has officially released the Technician Grade III answer key 2025 on its website, bsphcl.co.in. Candidates who appeared for the exam can now access and download the provisional answer key in PDF format using their login credentials. This release allows candidates to verify their answers and raise objections, if necessary, within the stipulated timeframe.The recruitment exam for the Technician Grade III posts was conducted across Bihar from July 11 to July 22, 2025. A total of 2,156 vacancies are to be filled through this recruitment drive. The organisation has activated the link to download the answer key and to submit objections, providing transparency in the evaluation process.How to download the BSPHCL Technician Grade III answer key 2025Candidates can follow these steps to check and download the BSPHCL Technician Grade III answer key 2025:Step 1: Visit the official BSPHCL website at bsphcl.co.in.Step 2: On the homepage, locate and click the link titled “Inviting objection against model answer (Tech.Gr.-III, ENN-05/2024).”Step 3: Enter your application number, password, and the displayed captcha code in the respective fields.Step 4: Click the submit button to access the answer key.Step 5: Verify the details such as name, exam centre, and date, then download and print the answer key for future reference.Direct link to download the BSPHCL Technician Grade III answer key 2025Details of BSPHCL Technician Grade III recruitmentThe recruitment exam was conducted by BSPHCL to fill 2,156 Technician Grade III posts. Candidates appearing for the exam were provided the provisional answer key to cross-check their answers before the final evaluation. The provisional answer key enables candidates to raise objections in case of discrepancies or errors.The organisation will review all objections submitted online with the help of an expert panel. After considering these challenges, BSPHCL will release the final answer key. The results for the Technician Grade III posts will be declared after the final answer key is published. It is important to note that no objections can be raised against the final answer key.What's next after the answer key release?Following the evaluation of all objections, the final answer key will be issued by BSPHCL. Candidates are advised to keep a close watch on the official website for updates regarding the final answer key and the result announcement. Using the login credentials will be necessary throughout this process.For further details and to download the BSPHCL Technician Grade III answer key 2025, candidates should visit bsphcl.co.in.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
