Anil Kapoor shared a touching message on social media to celebrate the fourth wedding anniversary of his daughter, producer Rhea Kapoor, and filmmaker Karan Boolani. He posted a collection of rare photos showcasing the couple’s cherished moments, highlighting the love and close-knit family connections they share.Instagram tribute to the coupleTaking to Instagram, Anil shared a few adorable photos and wrote, “Happy Anniversary, @rheakapoor & @karanboolani ! Four years have flown by in a blur of laughter, love, family time, and the two of you always giving your best to your friends and teammates. Together, you are a rare strength in today’s world, a bond we deeply admire. Here’s to you both, and the beautiful life you’ve built, a source of pride and joy for our entire family!".Anand Ahuja’s birthday wishes from Anil KapoorAnand Ahuja, Sonam Kapoor’s husband, celebrated his birthday on July 29. He received many warm wishes from fans, friends, and family. One special message came from his father-in-law, Anil Kapoor, who shared heartfelt photos of the family on social media. Anil called Anand a “hands-on dad” and the “heart of their family.” The pictures showed Anil and Anand dressed alike, happy moments of Sonam and Anand, and Anand with their son, Vayu. The post ended with family photos that fans loved. The caption read, “Happy Birthday, Anand! From being the coolest sneakerhead to the most hands-on dad, you somehow manage it all without missing a beat (or dropping the ball)! You’re not just Sonam’s better half but truly the heart of our family. The love, calm, and chaos you bring is something we all cherish. Here’s to more hugs, dad drives, walks, and all the adventures you have with Vayu, your business wins, and family brunches! We love you and are lucky to call you ours, @anandahuja".Anil Kapoor’s upcoming projectsOn the work front, Anil Kapoor will star in ‘Subedaar’, an action drama directed by Suresh Triveni. The film is produced by Kapoor’s company AKFCN and Opening Image Films. Anil plays the main character, Subedaar Arjun Maurya, and Radhikka Madan is the lead actress. The movie is set in India’s heartland and will be available on Amazon Prime Video.
