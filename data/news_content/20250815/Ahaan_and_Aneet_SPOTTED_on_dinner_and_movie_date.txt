Amid the release frenzy of 'War 2' and 'Coolie', 'Saiyaara' co-stars Ahaan Panday and Aneet Padda were spotted enjoying a cosy date night together.According to viral clips doing the rounds online, the pair was spotted stepping out for a quiet dinner at a plush city restaurant, further igniting chatter about a possible relationship.Ahaan Panday and Aneet Padda’s chemistry steals the show at 'Saiyaara' success bashReportedly, the two also headed to a Mumbai theatre to catch the final screening of their blockbuster 'Saiyaara' on the big screen. In the video, Ahaan was seen playing the perfect gentleman, purchasing tickets for himself and Aneet before leading the way into the cinema hall, all while being trailed by paparazzi. The sighting only fuelled speculation that the sizzling on-screen romance between the two may have found its way into real life.Meanwhile, contradictory reports online state that the couple headed out to catch a screening of 'War 2', however, fans were convinced that they went to watch their movie after watching a video where the actor can be heard asking for 'Saiyaara' tickets.On the business front, 'Saiyaara', which has earned an estimated Rs 322 crore at the box office, is now nearing the end of its theatrical run. With the arrival of Hrithik Roshan and Jr NTR’s 'War 2' and Rajinikanth’s 'Coolie', the film’s collections have taken a sharp dip, falling to just Rs 25 lakh on its latest day, its first drop below the Rs 1 crore mark.While the production house is yet to make an official announcement, YRF casting director Shanoo Sharma added to the excitement by resharing post on her social media, revealing that 'Saiyaara' will begin streaming on Netflix from 12 September 2025."Get the latest news updates on Times of India, including reviews of the movie Coolie and War 2."About the AuthorTOI Entertainment DeskThe TOI Entertainment Desk is a dynamic and dedicated team of journalists, working tirelessly to bring the pulse of the entertainment world straight to the readers of The Times of India.
