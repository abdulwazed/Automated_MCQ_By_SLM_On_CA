In Bollywood, being a star kid doesn’t automatically guarantee immense wealth, and Hrithik Roshan’s journey proves that sustained success takes more than just blockbuster roles. The 50-year-old actor, who first wowed audiences with 'Kaho Naa… Pyaar Hai', has built a fortune that now reportedly stands at a staggering Rs. 3100 crore, making him the richest star kid in the industry. This immense fortune is not solely from his acting career, but largely driven by his successful sportswear brand, HRX, and a series of strategic investments.Here's a closer look at the 'War 2' star's net worth.Hrithik Roshan flaunts sculpted physique in unfiltered 'War 2' prep workout clipHrithik Roshan's fortuneAs reported by GQ India, The biggest contributor to Roshan's wealth is his sportswear and lifestyle brand, HRX. Launched in 2013, the brand has become a massive success, with a reported valuation of around Rs 7,300 crore. This venture has been a game-changer, establishing a new model for celebrity-led businesses in India by focusing on authenticity and a community-driven approach.Hrithik Roshan's blockbuster films and high-value endorsementsRoshan's acting career remains a crucial part of his income. He reportedly commands a hefty fee for his film roles, with reports stating he was paid Rs. 85 crore for his film 'Fighter' and is set to earn between Rs. 48 and Rs. 80 crore for 'War 2.' The actor is also expected to step behind the camera for 'Krrish 4', marking his directorial debut. Beyond the big screen, he has also leveraged his massive social media following of over 47 million on Instagram to secure lucrative endorsement deals with major companies, further amplifying his brand value.Hrithik Roshan's smart investments and luxurious assetsIn addition to his professional ventures, Hrithik's wealth is tied to smart real estate investments. He owns a sprawling sea-facing Juhu home valued at approximately Rs. 100 crore and a tranquil farmhouse in Lonavala, worth an estimated Rs. 33 crore. These tangible assets, combined with his successful entrepreneurial efforts, ensure a continuous flow of wealth and solidify his financial status.Hrithik’s acting career remains the cornerstone of his financial empire. Fresh off 'Fighter', for which he was reportedly paid Rs 85 crore, he’s set to reprise his role in 'War 2' opposite Jr NTR and Kiara Advani, charging between Rs 48 and Rs 80 crore. The actor is also expected to step behind the camera for 'Krrish 4', marking his directorial debut.
