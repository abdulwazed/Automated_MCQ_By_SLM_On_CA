SSC releases SI Paper II answer keys and stenographer marks 2024 at SSC.gov.in SSC SI answer key 2024: The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the final answer keys for the Sub-Inspector (SI) in Delhi Police and Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) Examination, 2024 (Paper II), along with the response sheets of candidates. In a separate notice, SSC has also published the final marks of all candidates—qualified and non-qualified—for the Stenographer Grade C & D Examination, 2024.Both updates were issued on August 14, 2025, and are now available on the official SSC website, ssc.gov.in. Candidates can log in using their registered credentials to access and download the relevant documents within the specified timelines. The final answer keys and candidates’ response sheets for the SI in Delhi Police and CAPFs Examination (Paper II) have been uploaded following the declaration of the results on August 8, 2025. In a separate notice, SSC has also published the final marks of both qualified and non-qualified candidates for the Stenographer Grade C & D Examination, 2024, after the final results were announced on July 11, 2025.Final answer keys and response sheets available for SI paper IIAccording to the official notice (No. HQ-C1208/1/2024-C1/2), the final answer keys along with individual response sheets for Paper II of the SI in Delhi Police and CAPFs Examination, 2024, have been made available on the SSC portal from August 14, 2025 (06:00 PM).Candidates can access and download their answer keys and response sheets until August 29, 2025 (06:00 PM) by logging in with their registered credentials. After this deadline, the documents will no longer be accessible. The marks of both qualified and non-qualified candidates in this examination will also be published shortly on the Commission’s website.SSC uploads final marks for stenographer grade C & D examination 2024As per the notice (File No. E/7/2024-C-2-Section), SSC has uploaded the final marks of all candidates who appeared for the Stenographer Grade C & D Examination, 2024. The marks are accessible from August 14, 2025 (06:00 PM) to September 13, 2025 (06:00 PM).Candidates must log in using their Registered ID and Password on the official SSC website to view or download their scorecards. They are advised to take a printout of the marks during the given time frame, as no copies will be available after the specified period.Steps to check and download SSC SI final answer keys and stenographer marksStep 1: Visit the official SSC website at ssc.gov.in.Step 2: Click on the login link relevant to the respective examination.Step 3: Enter your Registered ID and Password to access your dashboard.Step 4: For SI Paper II, click on the link to view final answer keys and response sheets. For stenographer results, click on the marks link.Step 5: Download and print your final answer key/response sheet or scorecard, as applicable.Availability and login informationFor the SI Paper II examination, the final answer keys and response sheets are available through the login portal at:https://ssc.digialm.com:443//EForms/configuredHtml/32874/89701/login.htmlBoth documents—the answer keys for SI Paper II and the marks for Stenographer Grade C & D—must be downloaded within their respective deadlines, after which they will be inaccessible.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
