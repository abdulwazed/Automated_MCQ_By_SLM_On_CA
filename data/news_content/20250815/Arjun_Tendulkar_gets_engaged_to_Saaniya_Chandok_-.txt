The engagement marks a special moment for both families and has drawn warm attention from fans across social media.Who is Saaniya Chandok? Saaniya Chandok comes from one of Mumbai’s most prominent business families. She is the granddaughter of industrialist Ravi Ghai, whose Graviss Group owns popular ice cream brands like The Brooklyn Creamery and operates Baskin Robbins in India. Despite her high-profile family background, she has maintained a low public profile, quietly supporting family businesses while preparing for her life with Arjun.Saaniya Chandok, Arjun Tendulkar and Sara Tendulkar. (X)Arjun’s cricket careerArjun Tendulkar has been making his own name in cricket, following in his father’s footsteps while carving a unique path. A left-arm fast bowling all-rounder, he began his professional career with Mumbai in the 2020-21 season, making his T20 debut against Haryana. He later moved to Goa in the 2022-23 season, where he made his First-Class and List A debuts. In red-ball cricket, he has played 17 matches, scored 532 runs including one century and two fifties, and taken 37 wickets with one five-wicket haul and two four-wicket hauls. In List A cricket, he has played 17 matches, scoring 76 runs. Arjun has also represented Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League, featuring in five matches.Spot Saaniya in Sara Tendulkar’s photos Recent pictures shared on Instagram offer a rare glimpse into the Tendulkar household. They show Sachin Tendulkar performing a pooja at home, accompanied by his wife Anjali, daughter Sara, and Saaniya Chandok, who stands to Sara’s right wearing a green kurti.Sara Tendulkar with her future sister-in-law Saaniya Chandok (on Sara’s right, wearing a green kurti) and other friends and family.Sara Tendulkar has also shared several photos with Saaniya on her Instagram, suggesting that the two may be close friends.
