Rajinikanth's 'Coolie' is off to a roaring start, grossing Rs 76.78 crore net in India after two days. (Picture Courtesy: Facebook) Rajinikanth’s latest blockbuster, 'Coolie', continues to draw massive crowds, starting its second day with a total India net of Rs 76.78 crore.The film, which opened on Thursday to thunderous response, earned an estimated Rs 65 crore net on Day 1 across languages.As reported by the Sacnilk website, Tamil is leading at Rs 45 crore, followed by Telugu at Rs 15 crore, Hindi at Rs 4.5 crore, and Kannada at Rs 50 lakhs.On Day 2 (Friday, August 15, 2025), Coolie added Rs 11.78 crore to its tally.Coolie | Song - I Am The Danger (Lyrical)PollWill 'Coolie' surpass the Rs 100 crore mark by the end of the extended weekend?YesNoTamil leads the charge with over 63% occupancyThe film’s Tamil version continues to dominate the charts. It has recorded an overall 63.86% occupancy in theaters on Friday as per early eastimates. Morning shows alone saw packed crowds. This is particularly in Chennai, Madurai, and Coimbatore.The Hindi version, titled 'Coolie: The Powerhouse', clocked an overall 30.23% occupancy. The metropolitan areas like Mumbai and Delhi are performing better than expected.Telugu audiences also turned up in large numbers. The area has a robust 68.14% occupancy on Day 2. Andhra Pradesh and Telangana turned out to be key contributors to the film’s pan-India success.Star-studded cast fuels pan-India pullThe film is directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. 'Coolie boasts a powerhouse ensemble featuring Rajinikanth, Nagarjuna Akkineni, Shruthi Haasan, Upendra, Sathyaraj, Soubin Shahir, and Aamir Khan. Independence Day weekend will be crucial in determining the film’s long-term box office run. Sunday advance bookings are looking strong.
