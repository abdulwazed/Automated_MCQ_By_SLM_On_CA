Akshay Kumar celebrated India's 79th Independence Day with a heartfelt message on Instagram, showcasing his patriotic spirit. Known for films like 'Holiday,' 'Baby,' and 'Kesari' that evoke national pride, he won a National Award for 'Rustom.' Kumar is now preparing for 'Jolly LLB 3,' a courtroom comedy-drama, where he will reunite with Arshad Warsi for a humorous legal showdown.

On the occasion of India’s 79th Independence Day, Bollywood star Akshay Kumar took to his Instagram Stories to share his warm wishes with fans and fellow countrymen. The actor posted a striking photo of India Gate adorned with the national flag and added a heartfelt message that read, “Happy Independence Day. Here’s to the freedom that defines us and the pride that unites us.
