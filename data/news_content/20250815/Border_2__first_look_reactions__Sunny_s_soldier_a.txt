On the occasion of 79th Independence Day, fans of Bollywood’s most iconic war drama got the gift they’ve been waiting for, the first look of 'Border 2'. Sunny Deol, dressed in full army gear, a bazooka slung over his shoulder, and his trademark fiery glare locked in place, looks every bit the war hero audiences fell in love with back in 1997’s Border. Sharing the image on Instagram, Sunny wrote, “Hindustan ke liye ladenge… phir ek baar!”, setting the tone for another patriotic blockbuster.Fans flood social media with love for 'Border 2'Within minutes, social media exploded with reactions.
