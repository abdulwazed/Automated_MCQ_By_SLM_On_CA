AP EAMCET 2025 final allotment result out  AP EAMCET 2025 result: The Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) has released the final phase seat allotment result for the Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test (AP EAMCET) 2025. The results are now available on the official website at eapcet-sche.aptonline.in. Candidates who appeared for the exam can check their allotment status and download their seat allotment letter from the portal.The seat allotment list covers admissions to engineering and pharmacy courses for the 2025 academic session. Candidates allotted seats must complete the admission formalities by reporting to their respective colleges before the deadline. Allotment status can be verified using candidates’ hall ticket number and date of birth.How to check the AP EAMCET 2025 final allotment resultCandidates can follow the steps below to check and download their AP EAMCET 2025 final seat allotment result:Step 1: Visit the official website at eapcet-sche.aptonline.in or directly access the AP EAMCET 2025 portal.Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link labelled ‘AP EAMCET 2025 seat allotment’.Step 3: Enter the required login credentials, including your KEAM 2025 hall ticket number and date of birth.Step 4: Press the ‘Submit’ button to view your allotment status.Step 5: Download and save the seat allotment letter for future reference.Important details for candidates after seat allotmentCandidates who have been allotted seats must report to their respective colleges by August 20, 2025, with all necessary documents to complete the admission process. The documents required include the AP EAMCET rank card, hall ticket, proof of date of birth such as SSC or equivalent certificate, memorandum of marks for intermediate or equivalent, transfer certificate, and study certificate from class VI to intermediate.Direct link to check and download the AP EAMCET 2025 final seat allotment result onlineOther essential documents include the EWS certificate valid for the academic year 2025-26, residence certificate, Andhra Pradesh residence certificate, integrated community certificate, income certificate, and local status certificate where applicable.Spot round counselling if seats remain vacantIf any seats remain vacant after the final allotment round, APSCHE will conduct a spot round counselling session to fill the remaining seats. Candidates are advised to regularly check the official website for updates regarding this additional counselling phase.All candidates must ensure that they follow the guidelines carefully and report to their allotted institutions within the stipulated timeline to confirm their admission under the AP EAMCET 2025 counselling process.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
