Anirudh Ravichander composed the music. The action drama tells a revenge story. The film maintains good weekday collections.  Superstar Rajinikanth's 'Coolie' hit the theatres on August 14. Fans thronged the theatres in large numbers from the very first show.It was like a festival with the fans celebrating, crackers, and milk pouring everywhere. Expectations were high for ‘Coolie’ as it was Rajinikanth’s 50th anniversary film. Directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, the film has received decent reviews from critics and fans alike and has achieved huge box office collections upon release.Coolie Movie Review‘Coolie’ nets ₹193.25 crore in first four days'Coolie' has earned ₹193.25 crore in India net collection in the first four days of its release as per Sacnilk.The film collected ₹65 crore on the first day alone, Thursday. The Tamil version collected the most, earning ₹44.5 crore. The Hindi version collected ₹4.5 crore, Telugu ₹15.5 crore, and Kannada ₹0.5 crore. Although the second day collection decreased slightly, the film earned ₹54.75 crore. The film collected ₹39.5 crore on the third day, Saturday, and ₹34 crore on the fourth day, Sunday, making it a huge box office success in its first weekend.Day 5 collection pushes total to ₹196.55 croreOn its fifth day, Monday (Aug 18), 'Coolie' earned around ₹4.3 crore in India net collection. This brings the total India net collection to ₹196.55 crore. The film has maintained good collections even on weekdays, and fans are confident that 'Coolie' will set a record in the long run. The film is expected to cross ₹200 crore by the end of the day. If it continues at this pace, there is a good chance that ‘Coolie’ will also set a huge record at the global box office.Star-studded cast and Anirudh’s music power the filmNagarjuna Akkineni, Aamir Khan, Sathyaraj, Shruti Haasan, Soubin Shahir, Rachita Ram, and Upendra, among others play crucial roles in 'Coolie', and the film has music composed by Anirudh Ravichander. The action drama narrates a revenge story, and the highly anticipated film failed to surprise fans with its story and screenplay.
