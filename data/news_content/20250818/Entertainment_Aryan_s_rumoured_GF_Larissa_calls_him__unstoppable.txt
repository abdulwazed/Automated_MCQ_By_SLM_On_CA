Bollywood star Shah Rukh Khan’s son Aryan Khan is stepping into the world of Entertainment, but behind the camera. His directorial debut series, 'The Ba***ds Of Bollywood', has just dropped its first look, and the reactions are pouring in. And now Aryan’s rumoured girlfriend, Larissa Bonesi seems impressed. She didn’t hold back while praising him and his upcoming project. Larissa calls Aryan ‘unstoppable’ after teaserLarissa shared the teaser on her Instagram stories, writing, “Unstoppable, unmatched, and truly World’s #1! Proud is an understatement!” Aryan reshared her story, showing his appreciation for her support. Her message added a personal touch to the growing excitement around the series.SRK confirms cameo in 'Aryan Khan’s 'The Ba****ds of Bollywood'Earlier support proves her admirationThis isn’t the first time Larissa has shown support for Aryan’s work. In February, when Netflix officially announced the show, she shared a heartfelt note on Instagram. She wrote, “Thaaat’s fire! 'The Ba***ds of Bollywood'.” She also described the show as, “The most awaited show in the whole world. By the beast, the genius, and the number one, Aryan Khan.” Her excitement was clear, accompanied by a string of emojis celebrating Aryan’s dedication.Who is Larissa Bonesi?As reported by News18, born on 28 March 1990 in Brazil, Larissa Bonesi is a model and actress who has worked in Hindi and Telugu films. She featured as a dancer in Akshay Kumar and John Abraham’s 'Desi Boyz' song ‘Subah Hone Na De’. Larissa had a minor role in Saif Ali Khan’s 'Go Goa Gone' and appeared as the female lead in the Telugu action-comedy 'Thikka'.Aryan and Larissa’s dating rumoursFans began speculating about Aryan and Larissa’s relationship last year after noticing Aryan followed her and her mother, Renata Bonesi, on Instagram. Larissa reportedly follows the Khan family in return. The duo was also seen celebrating New Year 2025 together at a Mumbai party with other celebrities, fuelling further rumours.About 'The Ba***ds Of Bollywood''The Ba***ds Of Bollywood' is a fictional, self-aware dramedy set in the high-energy and unpredictable world of Indian cinema. Aryan Khan serves as the creator and director, working with co-creators and writers Bilal Siddiqi and Manav Chauhan.The star-studded cast includes Bobby Deol, Lakshya, Sahher Bambba, Manoj Pahwa, Mona Singh, Manish Chaudhari, Raghav Juyal, Anya Singh, Vijayant Kohli, and Gautami Kapoor.
