The Vardhman Mahaveer Open University (VMOU), Kota has announced the Rajasthan Pre-Teacher Education Test (PTET) 2025 round 2 seat allotment results today, August 18, 2025. Candidates who had successfully registered for counselling and completed the choice-filling process between August 10 and August 14, 2025, can now check their allotment status online on the official website: ptetvmoukota2025.in. The seat allotment has been carried out based on the candidate’s rank, submitted preferences, and availability of seats across participating institutions.How to check Rajasthan PTET 2025 round 2 seat allotment result Candidates can check and download their allotment letter by following these steps:  Visit the official PTET website: https://ptetvmoukota2025.in/   Click on the “Print Allotment Letter” link.   Enter your Roll Number, Counselling ID, and Date of Birth.   Download the allotment letter and take a printout for admission formalities.   Direct link to check the Rajasthan PTET 2025 round 2 seat allotment result is available here: Link 1, Link 2.What's next for candidates?  Once the allotment result is downloaded, candidates must complete the following admission procedures within the given deadlines:  Pay the admission fee: An amount of ₹22,000 has to be paid online through bank or e-Mitra by August 23, 2025.   Report to the allotted college: Candidates must physically report at their allotted institution with the required documents by August 25, 2025. Timely fee payment and physical reporting are mandatory to secure admission. Missing the deadlines may lead to cancellation of the allotted seat.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
