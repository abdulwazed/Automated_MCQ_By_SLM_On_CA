MP NEET UG 2025 round 1 seat allotment results to be released today at dme.mponline.gov.in: Check steps to download and other important details
The Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Madhya Pradesh, will release the MP NEET UG 2025 Round 1 seat allotment results today, August 18 at dme.mponline.gov.in. Candidates must report to allotted colleges between August 19–23, with options for seat cancellation till August 24 and upgradation till August 23. Over 5,800 MBBS/BDS seats are available across government and private colleges

Over 5,800 MBBS/BDS seats are available across government and private colleges  The Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Madhya Pradesh, will announce the Madhya Pradesh National Eligibility cum Entrance Test Undergraduate (MP NEET UG) 2025 Round 1 seat allotment results today, August 18, 2025. Candidates can access the allotment results on the official website: dme.mponline.gov.in.Once the MP NEET UG 2025 Round 1 allotment results are declared, candidates must report to their allotted colleges from August 19 to 23, 2025, according to the revised schedule. Students can also opt for seat cancellation until August 24 and seat upgradation until August 23.How to download MP NEET UG 2025 seat allotment resultCandidates can follow these steps to download their MP NEET UG 2025 seat allotment result in PDF format:Visit the official DME Madhya Pradesh website: dme.mponline.gov.in.Look for the link titled “MP NEET UG Counselling 2025 Seat Allotment Result”.Submit the required information, if any, to view your allotment result.Download and print the PDF for future reference and college reporting.MP NEET UG 2025: Seat allotment criteriaSeats are allotted based on:Merit rankPreferences filled by the candidateReservation criteriaSeat availabilityOther relevant factorsThe allotment list includes details such as the candidate's information, allotted institute, and allotted course.MP NEET UG 2025: Seat availabilityFor MBBS admissions, a total of 2,638 seats are available in government medical colleges and 2,200 seats in private medical colleges. Additionally, 1,170 BDS seats are offered in private dental colleges.MP NEET UG 2025: Important instructions for candidatesDME Madhya Pradesh has instructed that students opting for seat upgradation must submit an affidavit of Rs 500 regarding rural service and complete the seat leaving bond if the seat is not upgraded.Note: Candidates are also advised to keep their documents ready for verification and report to their respective colleges within the prescribed timeline.MP NEET UG 2025: Updates on withheld resultsThe results of 79 UG candidates were previously withheld due to a petition concerning exam centre issues in Indore and Ujjain. They were allowed to participate in the counselling process after the National Testing Agency (NTA) declared their results.
