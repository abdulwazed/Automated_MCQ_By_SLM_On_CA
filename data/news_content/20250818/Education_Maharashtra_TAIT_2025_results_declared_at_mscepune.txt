The Maharashtra State Examination Council (MSEC), announced the results of the Teacher Aptitude and Intelligence Test (TAIT) 2025, today, on August 18, 2025. Candidates can now access their scorecards through the council’s official website, mscepune.in. The TAIT examination, conducted by the Maharashtra State Pariksha Parishad, is a mandatory eligibility test for aspiring teachers in government and local body schools across the state. Out of 2,28,808 registrations, a total of 2,11,308 candidates appeared for the exam this year.Official data released by the council highlights the participation of 15,756 B.Ed. candidates, 1,342 D.El.Ed. candidates, and 17,098 applications for professional qualification verification.Maharashtra TAIT result 2025: Steps to download scorecardsCandidates can follow the steps mentioned here to download the Maharashtra TAIT result 2025 from the official website:Visit the official website: mscepune.inClick on the link titled “TAIT 2025 Result”Enter your roll number and password/date of birthView your result on the screenDownload the scorecard and take a printout for future referenceAlternatively, candidates can click on the link provided here to download the Maharashtra TAIT result 2025 from the official website.MAHA TAIT result 2025: Next steps for qualified candidatesThose who qualify through TAIT 2025 will proceed to subsequent rounds of the recruitment process, which include district-wise selection and document verification.
