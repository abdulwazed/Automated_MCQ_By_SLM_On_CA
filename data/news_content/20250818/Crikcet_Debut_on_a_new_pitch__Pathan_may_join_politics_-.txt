(@iamyusufpathan via PTI Photo)    *** Local Caption *** A year after his elder brother Yusuf Pathan joined politics and went on to win from the Baharampur Lok Sabha seat in West Bengal's Murshidabad district for the Trinamool Congress, younger brother Irfan Pathan, too, has shown interest in following his brother's path.Yusuf Pathan had defeated Congress veteran Adhir Ranjan Chowdhury, who had been winning the Baharampur constituency since 1999 and had consecutively won Lok Sabha elections.IRFAN PATHAN FLYING FROM MUMBAI SPOTTED AT AIRPORTGo Beyond The Boundary with our YouTube channel. (Do you think I should join?)"He then made it more specific and stated: “Mai jo bhi karoonga apni zindagi mey... aage jaa ke ya abhi hamesha difference paida karne ke liye karoonga aur agar nahi kar paaya toh nahi karoonga (Whatever I do in my life—whether now or in the future—I will always do it to make a difference. At his peak, he could swing the new ball prodigiously and contribute vital runs down the order, earning comparisons with Kapil Dev. His hat-trick against Pakistan in the 2006 Karachi Test remains historic.
