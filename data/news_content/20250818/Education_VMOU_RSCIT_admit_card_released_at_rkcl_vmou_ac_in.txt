VMOU RSCIT admit card 2025 released at rkcl.vmou.ac.in  VMOU RSCIT admit card 2025: Rajasthan Knowledge Corporation Limited (RKCL) has officially released the admit card for the Rajasthan State Certificate in Information Technology (RSCIT) examination scheduled for August 24, 2025. The exam is conducted by Vardhman Mahaveer Open University (VMOU), Kota, and the hall tickets are now available online at the official website: rkcl.vmou.ac.in.Candidates who have completed their RSCIT course and are appearing for the upcoming examination can now download their admit cards. The hall ticket is a mandatory document that must be presented at the examination centre to gain entry.Details available on the RSCIT hall ticketThe RSCIT admit card contains important information such as the candidate’s name, registration number, photograph, exam centre address, and the reporting time. Students are advised to verify all details carefully before printing the admit card. Any discrepancy should be reported immediately through the official contact channels provided by VMOU.Exam centres across three divisionsThe RSCIT exam on August 24, 2025, will be conducted across designated districts in three major divisions of Rajasthan—Jodhpur, Kota, and Udaipur.In the Jodhpur Division, the exam will be held in the districts of Jodhpur, Barmer, Jaisalmer, Jalore, Pali, and Sirohi.In the Kota Division, candidates from Kota, Baran, Bundi, and Jhalawar will take the test.In the Udaipur Division, the exam will be conducted in Udaipur, Banswara, Chittorgarh, Dungarpur, Pratapgarh, and Rajsamand.Candidates from these areas must check their hall ticket for the exact exam venue, reporting time, and instructions.Importance of the RSCIT admit cardThe RSCIT hall ticket serves as an official entry pass to the examination centre. Without this document, students will not be permitted to appear for the exam. It also acts as a form of identification and ensures that the candidate appears at the correct centre as per the schedule. The document should be preserved carefully until the conclusion of the examination process.Steps to download VMOU RSCIT admit cardFollow these steps to access and download your RSCIT hall ticket from the official website:Step 1: Visit the official RKCL website: rkcl.vmou.ac.inStep 2: Click on the link titled "RSCIT Admit Card 2025"Step 3: Select your district and enter your roll number or name and date of birthStep 4: Click on the "View" or "Submit" button to display your admit cardStep 5: Download the admit card and take a clear printout for exam day useDirect link to download the VMOU RSCIT Admit Card 2025Students are strongly advised to check their details thoroughly and reach the examination centre well in advance on the exam date.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
