PSSSB Group B recruitment 2025: Full details on vacancies, eligibility, and how to apply The Punjab Subordinate Services Selection Board (PSSSB) has officially announced the recruitment process for various Group B posts under Advertisement No. 05/2025. A total of 368 vacancies are available, covering roles like Senior Assistant, Junior Auditor, District Treasurer, Treasury Officer, Sub Divisional Officer (Civil), and Section Officer (Civil & Electrical). Eligible candidates can now apply for these positions via the official PSSSB website, sssb.punjab.gov.in.This recruitment initiative provides a golden opportunity for individuals seeking government jobs within the state of Punjab. The application process will be conducted online, and interested candidates must ensure they meet the qualifications and other requirements stipulated in the official notification. The application window is open for a limited time, with a clear deadline for submission.Available vacancies and educational qualificationsPSSSB has advertised a range of Group B posts across different sectors. The positions and their corresponding educational qualifications are as follows:• Senior Assistant (245 vacancies): Graduate with a 120-hour IT course and a typing test• Junior Auditor (Local Audit) (62 vacancies): B.Com or M.Com• Junior Auditor (Treasury & Accounts) (14 vacancies): B.Com or M.Com with a typing speed of 30 WPM in English• District Treasurer (1 vacancy): Graduate• Treasury Officer (36 vacancies): Graduate• Sub Divisional Officer (Civil) (2 vacancies): B.E./B.Tech (Civil) or a Diploma with relevant experience• Section Officer (Civil) (4 vacancies): Diploma or Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering• Section Officer (Electrical) (3 vacancies): Diploma or Bachelor’s degree in Electrical EngineeringCandidates must meet the specific educational qualifications for each post in order to be considered eligible.Age limit and relaxation categoriesThe minimum age for all posts is 18 years, and the upper age limits are categorised as follows:• General category: 37 years• SC/BC of Punjab: 42 years• State & Central Govt employees: 45 years• Ex-Servicemen (Punjab Domicile): Service years deducted from age + 3 years (maximum 45 years)• PwD (Punjab Domicile): 47 years• Widows/Divorcees/Others (Female Categories): Up to 40 yearsImportant dates and application feeCandidates can apply from July 22, 2025, until August 18, 2025. The last date for fee payment is August 20, 2025. The application fee varies by category:• General/Freedom Fighters/Sports: Rs 1000• SC/BC/EWS: Rs 250• Ex-Servicemen & Dependents: Rs 200• PwD (Divyang): Rs 500Selection processCandidates will be selected through a written examination, which may be followed by a skill test for specific roles, such as Senior Assistant and Junior Auditor (Treasury). Document verification will be conducted for shortlisted candidates, with a final merit list being published after the completion of all stages.How to apply online for PSSSB Group B Recruitment 2025To apply for the PSSSB Group B Recruitment 2025, follow these steps:Visit the official website: Go to sssb.punjab.gov.in.Register: Create an account using a valid email ID and phone number.Fill in the application form: Provide all required personal, educational, and experience details.Upload documents: Upload scanned copies of your photograph, signature, and relevant documents.Pay the application fee: Pay using online methods like Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI.After completing the application process, submit the form and keep a printout for future reference.
