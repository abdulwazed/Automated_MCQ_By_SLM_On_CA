Priyanka Chopra Jonas took fans on a nostalgic ride as she revisited her 2009 hit Kaminey, sharing fond memories with co-star Shahid Kapoor and director Vishal Bhardwaj. While her heartfelt post celebrated the film’s legacy, it also reignited fans’ longing for her long-overdue return to Bollywood.How Kaminey came her wayAlong with sharing glimpses of her character Sweety Bhope and Shahid Kapoor’s Guddu from Kaminey, Priyanka Chopra recalled being offered the film while she was in Miami shooting for Dostana (2008). Priyanka Chopra’s no-makeup look at Hyderabad airport leaves fans shockedCheck out the post here:Revealing how Vishal Bhardwaj flew down to meet her, PeeCee shared, “I remember him telling me the story and I said, “Well, she has approximately 8 scenes.” And he said, “With us working on it, it will end up being so much more. Trust me.” And I did. He promised he would make something incredible for me after this in appreciation for doing the part. But honestly, I was just greedy to work with him. A few years later, we did 7 Khoon Maaf.” A turning point in her careerGiving a special shout-out to the team and her co-star Shahid, Priyanka shared, “#Kaminey remains a turning point in my career. I learned so much from the master @vishalrbhardwaj, how to research, prepare and then surrender to the part. @shahidkapoor was sensational in his double role. #AmolGupte was unforgettable. I also met @mubinarattonsey for the first time on this set. ❤️ Those were the times. 16 years ago!”Fans want Priyanka back in BollywoodAs soon as she shared the post, likes and comments poured in from all sides. While one fan wrote, 'Watching these snippets just makes us realise what Bollywood is missing out on. The way its been feeding us mediocrity left right and centre these days. Laut aao PC!', another one added, 'Probably one of the finest actress and till today i see so many movies and feel Priyanka would have nailed it in that particular role. We as a generation truly miss your presence in Bollywood'.A fan also commented, 'Please come back to Hindi cinema and yes watched all your movies.. You are always incredible!'On the work front, Priyanka will next be seen in SSMB29, with SS Rajamouli and Mahesh Babu.
