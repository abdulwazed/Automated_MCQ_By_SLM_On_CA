'Coolie' vs 'War 2': Rajinikanth's film leads Jr NTR-Hrithik Roshan's film
Rajinikanth's 'Coolie' has gained a slight advantage over 'War 2' at the box office, particularly in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. 'Coolie' earned Rs 12 crore in the Nizam region over its opening weekend, surpassing 'War 2' which collected around Rs 10 crore. All India collections also show 'Coolie' leading with Rs 194.25 crore, while 'War 2' amassed Rs 173.60 crore.

Picture Credit: X Rajinikanth's 'Coolie' is enjoying a slight edge at the box office over 'War 2', particularly in the crucial Andhra Pradesh and Telangana markets. The Lokesh Kanagaraj directorial, which was released alongside the Hrithik Roshan–Jr NTR starrer, has emerged as the stronger contender in the Nizam region over its opening weekend.Extended weekend numbers in NizamAs per a report by 123Telugu, the film grossed an impressive Rs 1.6 crore on Sunday, its fourth day in theaters. This pushed the extended weekend total to a solid Rs 12 crore from all languages in Nizam alone. While Sunday's earnings dipped slightly compared to Saturday's Rs 2.65 crore share, the film's overall performance shows its clear hold over audiences.Hrithik calls Rajinikanth his 'first teacher' ahead of 'War 2' vs 'Coolie' clash'War 2' yet to match the paceOn the other hand, 'War 2', one of Bollywood’s most ambitious action spectacles featuring Hrithik Roshan and Jr NTR, has struggled to keep up in the region. Its four-day Nizam collection has been pegged at around Rs 10 crore, falling short of Rajinikanth's box office numbers. With both films now stepping into the working week, it remains to be seen whether 'Coolie' can maintain its strong momentum or if 'War 2' will make a late push.See More:'Coolie' box office collection day 5 (Live): Rajinikanth starrer holds strong, to surpass Rs 200 crore in IndiaAll-India collectionsAccording to trade analyst Sacnilk, the box office collections for the 2025 films 'Coolie' and 'War 2' reveal a strong showing for both, with 'Coolie' leading the race.'Coolie,' netted approximately Rs 194.25 crore in India, with day-wise earnings of Rs 65 crore on Day 1, Rs 54.75 crore on Day 2, Rs 39.5 crore on Day 3, and Rs 34 crore on Day 4. Meanwhile, 'War 2' performed well, it faced mixed reviews and comparatively slower growth in overall box office collections than 'Coolie.' Meanwhile, 'War 2', raked in Rs 52 crore on its first day. The film picked up further on Friday, collecting Rs 57.35 crore. However, Saturday saw a sharp dip with earnings of Rs 33.25 crore. On Sunday, the film added another Rs 31 crore as per early estimates.
