CGBSE Supplementary Result 2025: When will the CG Board Class 10 and 12 results be released? CGBSE Supplementary Result 2025: The Chhattisgarh Board of Secondary Education (CGBSE) is set to declare the Class 10 and 12 supplementary examination results for 2025 shortly. Students who appeared for the CGBSE supplementary examinations in July 2025 are advised to stay alert, as the results are expected to be released online soon via the official websites — cgbse.nic.in and results.cg.nic.in.The supplementary examinations were conducted for students who were unable to score the minimum passing marks of 33% in one or two subjects in the regular board exams. The Class 10 exams took place from July 9 to July 21, while the Class 12 exams were held from July 8 to July 22, 2025, in offline mode.CGBSE supplementary result 2025 date and timeThe board has not yet officially announced the exact date and time of the result declaration. However, based on previous years' trends, the results are expected to be declared in the latter part of August 2025. Students are advised to keep their admit cards ready and regularly check the official websites for updates. The results will be made available on cgbse.nic.in and results.cg.nic.in.Details mentioned on the CGBSE supplementary result 2025Once released, the CGBSE supplementary result 2025 will include the following details:• Student's name• Roll number• School name• Subject-wise marks• Total marks• Percentage• Result status (pass/fail)Candidates must carefully verify all the information on the scorecard. In case of any discrepancies or errors, they should immediately contact the board or concerned authority for rectification.How to check and download the CGBSE supplementary result 2025Students can follow the steps below to check and download their supplementary results once they are released:Step 1: Visit the official website of CGBSE at cgbse.nic.in.Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link that says "CGBSE Class 10/12 Supplementary Result 2025".Step 3: Enter your roll number and captcha code as displayed on the screen.Step 4: Click on the "Submit" button to proceed.Step 5: Your result will be displayed on the screen. Download and take a printout for future reference.Direct link to check the CGBSE Class 10 and 12 Supplementary Result 2025Websites to check CGBSE Class 10 and 12 resultsStudents can access their Class 10 and Class 12 supplementary results on the following official websites:• cgbse.nic.in• results.cg.nic.inIt is recommended to check the websites frequently for any official notification related to the result declaration.Examinations were held in July 2025The supplementary examinations were conducted for students who failed to pass one or two subjects in the main board examination.
