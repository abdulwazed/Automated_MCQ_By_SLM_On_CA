Arjun Tendulkar has become the talk of social media after news of his engagement to Saaniya Chandok went viral. The young cricketer, son of Indian legend Sachin Tendulkar, is part of the Mumbai Indians set-up in the IPL and made his debut for the franchise in 2023. Saaniya comes from a well-known business family in Mumbai. She is the granddaughter of entrepreneur Ravi Ghai, whose family has built a strong legacy in the hospitality and food industries, owning the InterContinental hotel and Brooklyn Creamery. The engagement was kept private, away from the glare of cameras and lavish parties. At 25, Arjun is still shaping his cricket career. With Mumbai Indians, he has featured in five IPL matches so far. His only batting opportunity saw him strike a six and score 13 off nine balls, while with the ball he has claimed three wickets. For the Tendulkar family, this is a moment of joy and celebration.
