BSSC CGL 4 graduate level recruitment 2025 BSSC CGL 4 graduate level recruitment 2025: The Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC) has officially launched its recruitment drive for 2025, offering a total of 5208 vacancies for graduate and office peon positions. The recruitment is part of the BSSC's Combined Graduate Level (CGL) 4 examination, which will fill various posts across multiple departments in the Bihar state government. This comes after the recent announcements and approvals from the General Administration Department of Bihar. The BSSC has confirmed that the online application process for graduate-level posts commenced on August 18, 2025, and will remain open until September 19, 2025.Candidates can apply for these positions via the official BSSC website at bssc.bihar.gov.in, where they will be able to fill out their application forms and pay the relevant examination fees. The recruitment covers both graduate level and office peon posts, with various positions available across different departments.Available positions in graduate and office peon categoriesThe BSSC recruitment drive for 2025 offers a total of 5208 positions, divided into two primary categories: graduate-level posts and office peon posts.For graduate-level positions, a total of 1481 vacancies are available. These include 1064 posts of Assistant Branch Officer in the General Administration Department, 88 posts of Planning Assistant, 5 posts of Junior Statistical Assistant, 1 post of Data Entry Operator, 125 posts of Auditor, and 198 posts of Auditor Cooperative Societies.In addition to graduate posts, the recruitment also includes 3727 vacancies for office peon roles in various departments. Among the departments offering peon positions are the Directorate of Planning and Training, Labour Resources Department, Road Construction Department, and Transport Department, among others. Notably, 221 posts are available in the Collectorate Patna, and there are significant vacancies across multiple districts, including Muzaffarpur, Katihar, Nalanda, and more.Important dates for online applicationOnline applications for graduate-level posts will be accepted from August 18, 2025, to September 19, 2025. For office peon posts, candidates can submit their applications between August 25, 2025, and September 26, 2025.Candidates wishing to apply for these positions must complete their application forms on the official BSSC website, making sure to upload all required documents and submit the examination fees by the deadlines.How to apply for BSSC CGL 4 graduate level recruitment 2025Follow these five steps to successfully apply for the BSSC CGL 4 graduate level recruitment 2025:1. Visit the official BSSC website: Go to bssc.bihar.gov.in.2. Register your details: If you are a new user, click on the “New Registration” link, and provide your basic details such as name, date of birth, email, and contact information.3. Fill in the application form: After successful registration, log in to your account and fill in the application form. Ensure all fields, such as your educational qualifications and personal details, are entered accurately.4. Upload documents: Upload scanned copies of the required certificates, including educational qualifications, caste certificates, EWS certificate, and any other documents as specified by the commission.5. Pay the examination fee: Once your application is complete, proceed to pay the examination fee online using one of the available payment options like credit card, debit card, UPI, or net banking.After submission, review your application to ensure all information is correct, then click on "Submit" to finalise your application.The BSSC CGL 4 graduate level recruitment 2025 presents a significant opportunity for candidates looking to secure a position in the Bihar state government. With 5208 vacancies available across various departments, eligible candidates are encouraged to apply within the stipulated time frame to avoid any last-minute rush.
