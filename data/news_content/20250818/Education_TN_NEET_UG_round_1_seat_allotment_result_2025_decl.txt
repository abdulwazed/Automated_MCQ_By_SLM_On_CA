The Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Tamil Nadu, has released the NEET UG 2025 Round 1 seat allotment results for MBBS and BDS courses. Candidates can check their status at tnmedicalselection.net and download allotment orders until August 24, 12 noon. The reporting deadline is August 24, 5 PM. TN NEET UG 2025 Round 1 seat allotment result 2025: The Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Tamil Nadu, on Monday declared the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) UG 2025 Round 1 seat allotment results for MBBS and BDS courses. Candidates who participated in the state counselling process can access their allotment status on the official website — tnmedicalselection.net.According to the notification, students allotted seats in the first round will be able to download their allotment orders until August 24, 2025, up to 12 noon. The deadline for reporting to the respective colleges is August 24, 2025, by 5 PM.Candidates seeking an upgrade will have the opportunity to participate in the second round of counselling, for which the detailed schedule will soon be released on the official portal.TN NEET UG 2025 Round 1 seat allotment result: Steps to download scorecardsCandidates can follow the steps mentioned here to download the TN NEET UG 2025 round 1 seat allotment result 2025 from the official website:Visit the official website at tnmedicalselection.net.Navigate to the Notifications section on the homepage.Click on the link titled “Provisional List of Candidates allotted for MBBS/BDS Government Quota/Management Quota Course Session: 2025-2026.”The seat allotment list will be displayed in PDF format.Download and take a printout of the allotment order for future reference.Alternatively, candidates can click on the link provided here to download the TN NEET UG round 1 seat allotment result 2025 for the government quota.Candidates can click on the link provided here to download the TN NEET UG round 1 seat allotment result for the Management quota.Aspirants are advised to stay tuned to the official website to get the complete details of the TN NEET UG counselling 2025.Ready to navigate global policies?
