Big B praised Ranbir as the "Superstar of the day," highlighting their mutual admiration.  Bollywood legend Amitabh Bachchan, known for his active presence on social media, often treats his followers to rare glimpses from his glorious career and personal moments. Back in 2017, the megastar shared a special throwback photograph that has since become one of the most cherished memories for fans. Captioning the post, he wrote, “T 2491 - And that wide eyed little fellow is the Superstar of the day today! Ranbir Kapoor .. !! what an actor !!”The heartwarming photo featured a young Ranbir Kapoor with his sister Riddhima Kapoor Sahni, holding the hand of their mother Neetu Kapoor, while speaking to Amitabh Bachchan. Adding more to the nostalgia, the late veteran actor Shashi Kapoor was also seen in the frame. Big B’s acknowledgment of Ranbir KapoorAmitabh Bachchan’s remark, calling Ranbir “Superstar of the day today”, connected deeply with audiences and with Ranbir himself. Social media lit up with fans celebrating the moment, noting how rare it is for Big B to publicly lavish such direct praise.Over the years, the two actors have shared mutual admiration. Amitabh has not only worked with Ranbir on-screen but has also encouraged his endeavors beyond films, always highlighting his talent, humility, and hard work.Fast-forward to May this year, when Ranbir Kapoor ventured into entrepreneurship with his lifestyle brand. Amitabh Bachchan once again extended his support in the most personal way. The actor received a pair of sneakers from Ranbir's brand and shared a handwritten note of gratitude. “Dearest Ranbir, My gratitude for your gift, the ARKS sneakers. Love tried them on and wore them to work. They are rather nice and comfortable! All good wishes to you and your endeavour. Lots of love (sic),” read Big B’s message.As for Ranbir’s cinematic journey, he will next be seen in 'Love and War' alongside Alia Bhatt and Vicky Kaushal. It is being directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, and is slated for Christmas 2025.
