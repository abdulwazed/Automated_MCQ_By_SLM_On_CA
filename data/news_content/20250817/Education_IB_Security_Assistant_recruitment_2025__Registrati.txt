The Intelligence Bureau (IB) under the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) has announced that the application window for the recruitment of Security Assistant/Executive will close today, August 17, 2025. Aspirants who meet the eligibility criteria must submit their applications through the official website, mha.gov.in, before the deadline.This recruitment drive aims to fill a total of 4,987 vacancies across various categories. Candidates who have completed Class 10 or possess an equivalent qualification from a recognised Board of Education are eligible to apply. The age requirement for applicants is between 18 and 27 years as on August 17, 2025.For candidates interested in positions within a specific Subsidiary Intelligence Bureau (SIB), proficiency in at least one of the designated languages or dialects for that SIB is mandatory. Candidates must be able to read, write, and speak the selected language fluently.How to apply for IB Security Assistant recruitment 2025Eligible candidates can follow these steps to complete their registration:Visit the official website: mha.gov.inClick on the "IB Security Assistant/Executive Recruitment 2025" link on the homepage.Complete the registration process and log in to the portal.Fill in the application form with all required details.Upload the necessary documents and pay the application fee.Submit the form and take a printout for future reference.Direct link to apply for IB Security Assistant recruitment 2025 is available here.IB Security Assistant recruitment 2025: Application feeThe application fee is ₹100, along with a recruitment processing charge of ₹550. Payments can be made through Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, UPI, or Challan.IB recruitment 2025: Category-wise vacanciesHere is the detailed distribution of vacancies across different categories:CategoryNumber of seatsUnreserved (UR)2,471Other Backward Classes (OBC)1,015Economically Weaker Section (EWS)501Scheduled Caste (SC)574Scheduled Tribe (ST)426Candidates are advised to complete their applications promptly as the registration window closes today.
