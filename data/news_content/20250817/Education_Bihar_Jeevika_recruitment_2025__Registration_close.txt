The Bihar Rural Livelihoods Promotion Society (BRLPS) has announced that the online application process for its 2025 recruitment drive will conclude tomorrow, August 18, 2025. Interested and eligible candidates must submit their applications through the official website, brlps.in, before the deadline. This recruitment exercise aims to fill a total of 2,747 vacancies across various posts, including Block Project Manager, Livelihood Specialist, Area Coordinator, Community Coordinator, and others. Applicants holding a Bachelor’s or Postgraduate degree are eligible to apply. Selection will be conducted through a combination of a written examination and a typing test, along with other evaluation stages where applicable.How to apply for Bihar Jeevika recruitment 2025 Candidates can complete their registration by following these steps:  Visit the official website: brlps.in   Navigate to the “Careers” or “Recruitment” section on the homepage.   Select the link for Bihar Jeevika Recruitment 2025 Apply Online.   Register using a valid email ID and mobile number.   Fill out the application form with personal, educational, and professional details.   Upload scanned copies of required documents, photograph, and signature.   Pay the application fee using online payment options.  Download and keep a printout of the submitted form for reference.
