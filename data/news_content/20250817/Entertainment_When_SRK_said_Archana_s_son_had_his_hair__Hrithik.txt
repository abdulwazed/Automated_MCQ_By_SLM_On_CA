(Picture Courtesy: Facebook) Years ago, at a lively birthday celebration for Aryan Khan at Shah Rukh Khan’s iconic Mannat residence, Archana Puran Singh experienced a moment that would become one of her favourite anecdotes.As reported by NDTV, attending the party with her family, including her husband Parmeet Sethi and their young sons, Archana found herself in the middle of playful observations from two of Bollywood’s biggest stars.Shah Rukh Khan wins his first National Award; celebs and fans celebrate the long-overdue honour'His hair is exactly like mine' — Shah Rukh KhanArchana recalled that as soon as they arrived, Shah Rukh Khan met her son Ayushmann and immediately commented, “His hair is exactly like mine.” Without another word, SRK moved on to greet other guests, leaving Parmeet Sethi shooting his wife a curious, almost suspicious glance. The light-hearted moment didn’t end there. Moments later, Hrithik Roshan, also at the party, came up to Ayushmann, took a good look at him, and declared, “His eyes are exactly like mine.” Cue another pointed stare from Parmeet.Family resemblance wins in the endLaughing as she retold the story to Ajay Devgn and Mrunal Thakur, who were visiting her home during promotions for 'Son of Sardaar 2', Archana was quick to clarify that despite SRK’s and Hrithik’s observations, both her sons — Ayushmann and Aaryamann — actually resemble their father, Parmeet Sethi. “They both look exactly like him,” she said, putting the playful speculation to rest. Mrunal Thakur, hearing the tale for the first time, was left in stitches, while Ajay Devgn smiled knowingly at the familiar mix of celebrity banter and family pride.Meanwhile, 'Son of Sardaar 2' received mixed reviews from the audiences.
