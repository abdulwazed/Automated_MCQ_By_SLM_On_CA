The West Bengal Board of Secondary Education (WBBSE) has officially released the admit card for the State Level Selection Test (SLST) 2025. This annual examination is a crucial step for candidates aspiring to secure government teaching positions in West Bengal. The release of the hall ticket marks the beginning of the final preparation phase for thousands of aspirants who have been rigorously studying for this highly competitive exam. Candidates are advised to download their admit cards promptly to avoid last-minute technical glitches. The admit card not only serves as a key to access the examination hall but also contains essential details like exam date, venue, roll number, and candidate information, making it indispensable for appearing in the test.How to download the SLST 2025 admit cardRegistered candidates can download their WBBSE SLST admit card by following the steps given below: Visit the official website: westbengalssc.com.Locate the “SLST 2025 Admit Card” link on the homepage.Enter your registration number and date of birth.Submit the details to view the admit card.Download and take a printout for examination purposes.Direct link to download here.Details mentioned on the admit cardCandidates should verify that the following information is correctly mentioned:Full name of the candidateRoll numberExamination center and addressDate and time of the examCandidate photograph and signatureExam day instructionsCandidates are advised to follow the instructions mentioned below for a smooth and successful conduct of the examination.
