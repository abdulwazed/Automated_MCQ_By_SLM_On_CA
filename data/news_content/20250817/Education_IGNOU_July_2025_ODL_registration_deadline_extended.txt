The Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has once again extended the registration and re-registration deadline for its July 2025 session, offering students more time to apply for Open and Distance Learning (ODL) programmes. The latest extension comes as a relief for fresh applicants as well as continuing students who faced difficulties in meeting the earlier deadlines. Candidates can now apply until August 31, 2025, through the official IGNOU portals.Deadline extension detailsIGNOU had initially set the deadline for fresh ODL admissions in July 2025 as July 15. This was extended to August 15, 2025, and now, in its latest notification, the university has confirmed that the new deadline for both fresh admissions and re-registration is August 31, 2025. The extension ensures that no learner is left out due to technical glitches, documentation delays, or other personal constraints.Direct links to applyFresh admission (ODL and online programmes): ignouadmission.samarth.edu.in Re-registration for continuing students: onlinerr.ignou.ac.inSteps to register onlineHere is how candidates can register online at the official IGNOU portal:Visit the portal – Use the appropriate link above for admission or re-registration.Register/Login – Create a new username and password if applying for the first time, or log in with existing credentials.Fill in details – Provide personal, academic, and programme-related information.Upload the following documents:Passport-size photograph (≤100 KB)Scanned signature (≤100 KB)Educational certificates (≤200 KB each)Category/experience certificates if applicablePay fees online – Through debit/credit card, net banking, or UPI. Eligible SC/ST students enrolling in UG ODL programmes may avail a 50% fee concession (if not already covered by other schemes).Submit and print confirmation – Save a copy for your records.Why this matters for studentsIGNOU’s extension ensures broader access for learners in rural and urban areas who rely on distance education to pursue higher studies without leaving their jobs or family responsibilities. With flexible admission policies and affordable fee structures, the university continues to support aspirants from diverse backgrounds, making higher education more inclusive.Ready to navigate global policies?
