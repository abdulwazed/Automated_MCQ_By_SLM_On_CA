Due to the sudden medical emergency, the idol’s next scheduled event, a fan meeting, has been cancelled until further notice.Jackson Wang has been hospitalisedJackson Wang was rushed to the hospital after he experienced discomfort, which was later revealed to be due to food poisoning, as per reports shared by ET Today. The idol’s company addressed the matter on August 16 and also shared the news of the event cancellation. “We’re so sorry to let you know that today’s Music Korea Fan Sign Event has to be cancelled,” they said in an official statement released online. The ‘BUCK’ singer has also been advised by the hospital to rest for proper recovery. In their official statement, the idol’s company also shared that whatever money has been spent on the ticket purchase will soon be credited back to the fan’s accounts. A new day and time for the event will be announced at a later date as well. About Jackson WangPart of the popular Korean group GOT7, Jackson Wang has managed to become a force to be reckoned with. The star is known for his powerhouse vocals as well as smooth dance moves, and has managed to garner a lot of praise and love from netizens around the world.The star dropped his latest album titled ‘Magic Man 2’ earlier this year on July 18, and has been praised by many for his new music. The release was headed by the drop of the new music video for one of the songs on the album, ‘Made Me a Man’.Wang’s schedule is jam-packed, as he is set to start off his world tour ‘Magic Man 2’ in October later this year.
