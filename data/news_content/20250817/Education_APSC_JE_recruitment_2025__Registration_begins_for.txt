The Assam Public Service Commission (APSC) has opened applications for 187 Junior Engineer (JE) positions across various engineering disciplines under the Public Health Engineering Department. This recruitment drive offers a significant opportunity for diploma holders in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Chemical Engineering to build a career in the government sector. With the increasing demand for skilled engineers in state infrastructure and public services, APSC’s recruitment comes as a timely initiative.Eligible candidates have until September 13, 2025, to submit their applications online. The selection process, age eligibility, educational qualifications, and application fee details have been outlined in the official notification, ensuring transparency and equal opportunity for candidates across Assam.APSC JE vacancy detailsAPSC has announced 187 Junior Engineer positions, distributed across multiple engineering disciplines as follows:Junior Engineer (Civil): 160 postsJunior Engineer (Mechanical): 10 postsJunior Engineer (Chemical): 10 postsJunior Engineer (Electrical): 7 postsEligibility criteriaAge Limit: Candidates must be between 18 and 40 years as of January 1, 2025. Age relaxation applies for reserved categories as per Assam government norms.Educational Qualification: Candidates must hold a three-year diploma in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, or Chemical Engineering from an AICTE-recognized institution. Diploma holders with practical knowledge of engineering projects are preferred.How to apply for APSC JE recruitmentAPSC has made the application process fully online to ensure convenience and transparency:Visit the official website: apsc.nic.inClick on the JE Registration 2025 link on the homepageRegister with your personal details and create a loginComplete the application form carefully, providing accurate educational and personal informationPay the application fee through the available online payment modesSubmit the application and download a copy for future referenceReady to navigate global policies?
