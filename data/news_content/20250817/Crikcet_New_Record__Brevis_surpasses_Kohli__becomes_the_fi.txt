Dewald Brevis and Virat Kohli (Agency Photos)  South African batter Dewald Brevis surpassed India's Virat Kohli in the record for most T20I sixes against Australia in Australia, becoming the first ever batter to hit 14 sixes in T20I Down Under. Despite his impressive performance, South Africa couldn't secure a victory in the series decider.Brevis scored a powerful 53 runs off 26 balls, which included one four and six sixes. His achievement of 14 sixes in just three matches overtook Kohli's record of 12 sixes in 10 T20Is against Australia in their home conditions.Shubman Gill as India’s all-format captain?
