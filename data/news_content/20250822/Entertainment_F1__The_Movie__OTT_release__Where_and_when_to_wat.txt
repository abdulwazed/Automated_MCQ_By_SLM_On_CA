Those who missed a chance to catch Brad Pitt's racing drama 'F1: The Movie' on the big screen, will now get the chance to watch it online as the film is making it OTT debut today. F1: The Movie OTT releaseThe film ignited the summer box office, amassing a global box office haul, estimated at $590.5 million since its theatrical release on June 27.
