Samsung Galaxy A35 5G, Galaxy A55 5G price revealed

At the time, the company only revealed the design and specifications of both the smartphones. And, now the official pricing has arrived for all the variants.Samsung has launched the Galaxy A35 5G in two variants -- 8GB + 128GB and 8GB + 256GB, while the Galaxy A55 5G comes in 8GB + 128GB, 8GB + 256GB and 12GB + 256GB variants.
