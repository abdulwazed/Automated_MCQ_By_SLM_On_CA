Elon Musk’s Starlink to use Aadhaar for seamless customer verification in India in collaboration with UIDAI

Starlink, under Elon Musk, is partnering with UIDAI to streamline KYC for Indian users via Aadhaar-based verification. This collaboration enables quick, secure, and paperless onboarding, ensuring regulatory compliance and a user-friendly experience. The initiative highlights the scalability and reliability of India's digital infrastructure, demonstrating how Aadhaar supports innovative technology adoption while maintaining transparency and user control over personal data.

Source: PIB Elon Musk’s Starlink Satellite Communications is set to transform India’s digital landscape by partnering with the Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) to leverage Aadhaar-based verification. This collaboration introduces a quick, secure, and fully KYC-compliant onboarding process for Indian users, enabling them to access high-speed satellite internet without cumbersome paperwork. By integrating Aadhaar e-KYC, Starlink ensures compliance with regulatory requirements while providing a smooth and convenient experience for households, businesses, and institutions. This partnership highlights the scalability and reliability of India’s digital infrastructure, demonstrating how Aadhaar can support innovative technology adoption while maintaining transparency, accountability, and user control over personal data.Starlink and UIDAI partnership: Aadhaar e-KYC for fast, safe, and secure onboarding experienceStarlink, founded by Elon Musk, has joined forces with UIDAI, the governing body for Aadhaar, to simplify KYC (Know Your Customer) procedures for Indian users. This collaboration allows Starlink to adopt Aadhaar-based verification, creating a fast, paperless, and secure onboarding experience tailored for both urban and rural communities.According to the Ministry of Electronics & IT, Starlink will implement Aadhaar e-KYC to meet regulatory compliance standards while ensuring a user-friendly verification process. This integration reduces administrative barriers, enhances digital trust, and enables households, businesses, and institutions to access satellite internet efficiently across diverse regions.How Aadhaar e-KYC simplifies onboardingAadhaar authentication is one of India’s most trusted digital identity solutions, utilising biometric and face recognition for instant verification.
