Ranbir Kapoor 'liked' Deepika Padukone’s old reel?

Ranbir Kapoor's brand, ARKS, stirred online buzz by allegedly 'liking' an old Instagram reel featuring Deepika Padukone and her sister, Anisha. Ranbir Kapoor does not have an official Instagram account, but recently netizens noticed some surprising activity online. His brand, ARKS, allegedly ‘liked’ an old reel featuring Deepika Padukone and her sister, Anisha Padukone. Reportedly, in the fun clip, the sisters are seen decorating a cake playfully, which immediately caught fans’ attention. The speculation is rife over whether Ranbir himself interacted or if it was just his team.Let's take a closer look at the alleged 'like' and the reel in question right here.Ranbir Kapoor playfully teases Alia Bhatt in front of the paps, ‘Yeh ruk nahi rahi'Ranbir’s brand account allegedly liked Deepika Padukone's postThe alleged interaction from Ranbir’s brand account has caused fans to speculate wildly. While some believe Ranbir personally liked the reel, others think it was done by his social media team by mistake. Either way, the post became a hot topic on X (formerly Twitter), especially given Ranbir and Deepika’s past and on-screen chemistry.Fans react with humour and playful commentsFans flocked to the comments section to acknowledge the alleged like which has now been deleted. On X, fans expressed their amusement and curiosity. One user joked, “Not Ranbir accidentally using his brand page instead of his spare account.” Another quipped, “The algorithm accidentally registered an interaction,” referring to a similar incident with Virat Kohli ‘liking’ Avneet Kaur’s post.Some fans simply said, “I’m just here for the drama,” one wrote, while another added, “Y’all are so weird. People are massively overreacting in the quotes. Must be liked by the admin of that page, weirdos.”Others found it sweet. One fan commented, “Okay, this is cute. He probably knows Anisha well, and this is totally harmless except for the fact that stan wars have made it ugly .” Another X post read, “Even if he or the admin liked it, why are these people crying in quotes lol.”Uncertainty over who liked the Instagram postEven with all the buzz, it’s impossible to know for sure whether Ranbir himself liked the Reel or if it was just his team. This uncertainty has kept fans talking, sharing memes, and joking online.Ranbir Kapoor's upcoming projectsWhile social media speculates, Ranbir Kapoor is focused on his professional work. He is currently busy with Nitesh Tiwari’s 'Ramayana.' The film boasts a stellar cast, including Yash, Amitabh Bachchan, Sai Pallavi, and Sunny Deol. Apart from this, he will also be seen in Sanjay Leela Bhansali's ‘Love and War’, starring wife Alia Bhatt and Vicky Kaushal.Deepika Padukone on the work frontDeepika Padukone is also keeping busy. She is preparing for Atlee’s upcoming sci-fi franchise, tentatively titled 'AA22xA6,' alongside Allu Arjun. In addition, she is reportedly set to appear in Shah Rukh Khan and Suhana Khan’s 'King.'
