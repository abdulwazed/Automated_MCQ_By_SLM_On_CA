Aishwarya Rai once compared Rajinikanth to Big B

Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, who shared screen space with Rajinikanth in 'Robot,' once drew parallels between him and her father-in-law, Amitabh Bachchan. She lauded both actors for their professionalism, humility, and legendary status, emphasizing that their dedication to their craft makes their films captivating for audiences. Both actors continue to be active in the film industry with recent and upcoming projects.

Both actors continue to be active in the film industry with recent and upcoming projects.  Rajinikanth and Amitabh Bachchan are two of the most iconic and beloved actors in Indian cinema history. Rajinikanth, often called the ‘Superstar’ of Tamil cinema, has a massive fan base not only in South India but across the country and abroad. Known for his unique style and larger-than-life screen presence, he has created an unmatched legacy. Amitabh Bachchan, the ‘Shahenshah’ of Bollywood, has been a towering figure for decades with his powerful acting and deep baritone voice. Both actors have millions of devoted fans who admire their work and charisma, making them true legends of Indian film. They have acted together in films like ‘Andhaa Kaanoon’, ‘Hum’, ‘Geraftaar’, and more recently in ‘Vettaiyan’. In an old interview with DNA, she said, “Rajini sir, like Pa (Amitabh Bachchan), is a professional and humility personified. There was no age factor. They are both legendary actors due to their body of work. Hence, their cinema becomes exciting to the viewers. Rajini sir's humility is a lesson to learn.”This shows how much respect she has for both actors, not just for their talent but also for their humility and professionalism.Rajinikanth’s recent projectsRajinikanth's latest film ‘Coolie’, directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj released in cinemas on August 14.
