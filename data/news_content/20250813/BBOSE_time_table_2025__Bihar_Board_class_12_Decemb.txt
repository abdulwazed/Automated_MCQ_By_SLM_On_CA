BBOSE class 12 December 2025 exam time table released, exams start from 25 August BBOSE class 12 December 2025 exam time table: The Bihar Board of Open Schooling and Examination (BBOSE) has released the time table for the Class 12 December 2025 examinations. As per the official schedule, the theory examinations will commence on August 25, 2025, and will continue across multiple days in two shifts.The examination will be conducted in two shifts daily. The first shift will be held from 9:30 AM to 12:45 PM, and the second shift will take place from 2:00 PM to 5:15 PM. Students are required to check their subject-wise schedule and adhere strictly to the allotted timings.Shift timings and entry rulesCandidates will be allowed to enter the examination centre only 30 minutes before the start of each shift. Entry for the first shift will close at 9:00 AM, while entry for the second shift will close at 1:30 PM. No candidate will be allowed to enter the centre after the stipulated time under any circumstances.An additional 15 minutes at the beginning of each exam shift will be allotted for students to read and understand the question paper. This reading time is included in the total duration of the examination.Facilities for disabled candidatesCandidates who are unable to write on their own due to disability will be permitted to use the services of a scribe or writer, as per the approval of the District Education Officer. As per the rules, students using a scribe will receive 20 minutes of compensatory time per hour of examination.BBOSE class 12 December 2025 theory exam scheduleBelow is the detailed subject-wise time table for the Class 12 December 2025 theory examinations:DateFirst shift (9:30 AM to 12:45 PM)Second shift (2:00 PM to 5:15 PM)August 25, 2025HindiEnglishAugust 26, 2025MathematicsChemistryAugust 27, 2025BiologyPhysicsAugust 28, 2025GeographyHistoryAugust 29, 2025MaithiliAccounting / Bangla / BhojpuriAugust 30, 2025Sanskrit / ArabicPsychologySeptember 1, 2025EconomicsHome ScienceSeptember 2, 2025Political ScienceSociologySeptember 3, 2025Yoga and Physical EducationComputer ScienceSeptember 4, 2025MusicDrawingSeptember 6, 2025Business StudiesPedagogySeptember 6, 2025Philosophy-September 8, 2025UrduMaghiPractical examination scheduleBBOSE has also announced the schedule for the Class 12 practical examinations.
