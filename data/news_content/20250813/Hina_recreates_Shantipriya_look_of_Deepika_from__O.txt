For the same, Hina decided to go for Deepika's Shantipriya avatar from Farah Khan's 'Om Shanti Om', starring Shah Rukh Khan. On the other hand, Rocky dressed like SRK's character Om from the film.Here’s a closer look. She wore a light pink ensemble and silver jewelry that complemented her attire. The actress exuded nothing but elegance. Hina also pinned pink roses in her hair, while Rocky was in a red checkered suit with black pants. The duo definitely pulled off their 'Om Shanti Om' avatars with grace. Check it out here.Soon, fans poured in love for the actress in the comment section of the video post.More about 'Om Shanti Om'The film marked Deepika Padukone's debut in Bollywood. The reincarnation love story received a lot of love from the masses and went on to become a hit. It was released in November 2007. The film also starred Shreyas Talpade, Kirron Kher, and Arjun Rampal.More about Hina KhanThe actress and her husband are currently part of the 'Pati Patni Aur Panga' show. It premiered on August 2, 2025. For the unversed, the actress has worked in movies like 'Hacked' (2020), 'Lines' (2020), 'Wishlist' (2020), 'Country of Blind' (2020), and 'Shinda Shinda No Papa' (2024).Meanwhile, in June last year, the actress publicly shared that she is suffering from stage-3 breast cancer.
