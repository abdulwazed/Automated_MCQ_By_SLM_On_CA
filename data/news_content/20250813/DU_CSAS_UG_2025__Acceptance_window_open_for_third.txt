CSAS UG 2025: DU announces third round allocation for CW, ECA, Sports and performance-based programmes NEW DELHI: The University of Delhi has declared the third round allocation for Undergraduate admissions under the Common Seat Allocation System (CSAS) 2025-26. This round covers performance-based programmes, ward quota, CW (Children/Wards of University employees), ECA (Extra-Curricular Activities), and sports categories. Candidates who participated in this round can now check their upgraded seat allocations on the official admission portal.The announcement was made at 5:00 pm on August 13, with candidates required to accept their allocated seats between August 13 and August 17. The university has set specific deadlines for fee payment and seat acceptance to ensure the smooth completion of the admission process.Key dates and admission scheduleThe third round of CSAS allocations includes performance-based programmes such as Music, BFA, PE (Physical Education), and HE&S (Health, Environment & Sports), along with the ward quota, CW, ECA, and sports categories. The round finalises the allotment of seats for these categories based on updated preferences and available vacancies.EventDate and timeDeclaration of third round allocationAugust 13, 5:00 PMAcceptance of allocated seatsAugust 13 to August 17Final date for fee paymentAugust 17, 4:59 PMColleges verify and approve applicationsAugust 13 to August 17Candidates who have been allocated seats must confirm their acceptance and complete the payment process by the stipulated deadlines. Failure to meet these requirements will lead to forfeiture of the allocated seat.Upgrade window and college verificationBefore the third round allocation, candidates admitted in earlier rounds were given the opportunity to upgrade their preferences during the upgrade and preference reorder window. This allowed candidates to opt for higher-preference programmes or colleges, with upgraded seat allocations processed automatically at the college level. Admission to an upgraded seat is confirmed only upon successful fee payment.Colleges are responsible for verifying and approving all online applications within the admission schedule. This ensures that candidates fulfil eligibility criteria and that all documentation is accurate. Verification for the third round admissions continues alongside seat acceptance until August 17.Payment deadlines and next stepsThe university mandates that candidates must pay the admission fees by August 17 to confirm their seat allocation. If the fee is not received by the deadline, the allocated seat will be cancelled and made available for further rounds or waiting-list candidates.Candidates are advised to regularly monitor the official admission website for updates and notifications. The university has also displayed a vacant seat matrix post this round to provide transparency and assist candidates in making informed choices.Direct link to the official website | Link 2Completion of these formalities marks the near conclusion of the admission process for CSAS (UG) 2025-26.
