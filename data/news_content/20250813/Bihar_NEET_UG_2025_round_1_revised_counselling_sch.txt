The Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) has released the revised schedule for the Bihar National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) Undergraduate (UG) counselling 2025 round 1. Candidates can register and complete the application-cum-choice filling process on the official website: bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.Candidates who were unable to register or complete the application and choice filling earlier can now submit their registrations until August 18, 2025. It is important to note that all choices filled between July 30 and August 8, 2025, have been declared null and void.Candidates who have already completed their registration and submitted applications within the stipulated period must undertake fresh choice filling according to the revised schedule. Failure to do so will result in non-allotment of seats during the counselling process.The Bihar NEET UG counselling 2025 had been temporarily paused after the board implemented a health department order requiring 50% of seats in private and medical colleges to follow the same fee structure as government colleges. In response, admissions to private institutions under the Bihar Graduate Medical Admission Counselling (UGMAC) 2025 will now proceed based on 100% of seats and their pre-determined fees, similar to the previous year.Revised Bihar NEET counselling 2025 schedule (Round 1)Candidates should note the revised schedule for Round 1 counselling as follows:EventDateRevised date for fresh online registration and submission of Application-cum-Choice filling of application formAugust 13, 2025Last date for online registration and submission of application form through Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking/UPI by registered candidatesAugust 18, 2025Last date of choice filling and lockingAugust 19, 2025Publication of Rank CardAugust 20, 2025Round 1 Provisional Seat Allotment Result publicationAugust 24, 2025Downloading of Allotment Order (Round 1)August 26 to 28, 2025Documents Verification and AdmissionAugust 26 to 28, 2025Alternatively, candidates can click on the link provided here to download the Bihar NEET UG 2025 round 1 revised counselling schedule.Candidates will also have the facility to edit their applications for Bihar NEET UG counselling 2025 after the registration and submission deadline.The UGMAC 2025 counselling process facilitates admissions to Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS), Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS), and Bachelor of Veterinary Science and Animal Husbandry (BVSc and AH) courses in Bihar.
