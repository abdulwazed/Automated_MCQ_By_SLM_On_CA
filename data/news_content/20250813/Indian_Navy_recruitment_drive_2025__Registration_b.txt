The Indian Navy has announced a major recruitment drive for 1,266 Civilian Tradesman Skilled positions, offering a significant opportunity for skilled workers to join one of the country’s most prestigious defence forces. The vacancies span multiple technical trades, ranging from electrical and electronics to shipbuilding and weapon systems. Eligible candidates, primarily 10th pass with ITI certification or relevant military/industrial training, can now apply online from today, August 13, 2025, with the last date being September 2, 2025. The positions come with a competitive pay scale of ₹19,900 to ₹63,200 per month, plus allowances, and selection will be through a written examination followed by skill tests. The recruitment aims to strengthen India’s naval capabilities with technically proficient civilian personnel.How to apply for Indian Navy recruitment drive 2025Here is how interested and eligible candidates can register online:Visit indiannavy.gov.inGo to the Recruitment section and click on “Civilian Tradesman Skilled 2025” linkRegister and fill in personal, academic, and trade preference detailsUpload scanned photograph, signature, and certificatesSubmit the form and save the confirmation pageDirect link to apply for the Indian Navy recruitment drive 2025 is available here.Indian Navy recruitment drive 2025: Vacancy overview The recruitment covers 1,266 posts distributed across trades such as:  Auxiliary   Civil Works   Electrical   Electronics & Gyro   Heat Engines   Ship Building   Weapon Electronics   These positions will be filled across various naval commands, ensuring technical readiness for diverse maritime operations.Indian Navy recruitment drive 2025: Minimum eligibility requirements Applicants must meet one of the following:  10th Pass with ITI certification in the relevant trade,   Completion of Apprenticeship Training at Naval Yard Apprentice Schools, or   Minimum two years of technical service in the Army, Navy, or Air Force.  Age limit: 18 to 25 years as of September 2, 2025.Age relaxation: Applicable for reserved categories as per government norms.Indian Navy recruitment drive 2025: Selection process The selection process for the Indian Navy’s Civilian Tradesman Skilled recruitment will be conducted in multiple stages to ensure only the most suitable candidates are chosen. It will begin with an initial screening of applications to verify eligibility and credentials. Shortlisted candidates will then appear for a written examination designed to assess their trade-specific knowledge as well as general aptitude. Those who qualify in the written test will be invited to undergo a skill or trade test, which will evaluate their practical proficiency in the chosen field. Finally, candidates who clear these stages will go through document verification and a comprehensive medical examination to confirm their fitness for service in the Navy’s demanding operational environment.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
