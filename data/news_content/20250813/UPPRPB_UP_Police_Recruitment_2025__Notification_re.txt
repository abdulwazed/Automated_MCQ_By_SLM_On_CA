UPPRPB UP Police Recruitment 2025: Notification released for over 4,500 SI posts; check exam pattern, syllabus, and how to apply online here
Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment 2025 invites applications for 4,543 Sub Inspector posts. Candidates must complete online registration by September 11, 2025. The exam covers Hindi, General Knowledge, Numerical Ability, and Reasoning with 160 objective questions. Detailed syllabus and exam pattern are available at UPPRPB.gov.in. Early preparation is advised to secure this prestigious role.

Uttar Pradesh Police has officially released a notification announcing 4,543 vacancies for the positions of Sub Inspector (Civil Police), Sub Inspector (Civil Police – Women) (PC), and Sub Inspector/Platoon Commander (Special Security Force). Aspiring candidates preparing for the Uttar Pradesh Police Sub Inspector Examination 2025 are advised to meticulously review the exam syllabus, examination pattern, and exam schedule to ensure targeted and effective preparation.Eligible candidates can apply online through the official portal at UPPRPB.gov.in.How to apply for UPPRPB UP Police Recruitment 2025Follow the detailed steps below to complete your online application.Visit the official website UPPRPB.gov.in.Click on the “UP Police SI Recruitment 2025” link on the homepage.Complete the new registration process by providing all mandatory details.Login to the candidate portal and accurately fill out the application form.Upload scanned copies of required documents.Pay the application fee through the available online payment methods.Submit the application form and retain a printed copy for future reference.Note: Applicants must first complete the One Time Registration (OTR) process to be eligible for submission. The application procedure is entirely online, and no submissions will be entertained after the closing date.
