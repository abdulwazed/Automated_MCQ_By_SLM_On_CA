Candidates can check results online using their application number and date of birth.

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has officially announced the results of the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) – All India Entrance Examination for Admission (AIEEA) Postgraduate (PG) and All India Competitive Examinations (AICE) – Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) / Senior Research Fellowship (SRF) Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) for the academic session 2025–26. Candidates can now access their scorecards on the official examination portal: exams.nta.ac.in/ICAR. The entrance examinations, held on July 3, 2025, were conducted in computer-based mode across 161 centres in 83 cities nationwide. According to NTA, 27,384 candidates registered for AIEEA PG, of which 25,338 appeared. For AICE JRF/SRF (Ph.D.), 11,116 candidates registered and 9,940 appeared. Following the objection window against provisional answer keys, NTA verified challenges and finalised the keys for result processing. In the final evaluation, nine questions from AIEEA PG and seventeen questions from AICE Ph.D. were dropped, and scores were computed accordingly.How to check ICAR AIEEA PG 2025 and AICE PhD 2025 resultCandidates can follow the steps below to access and download their scorecards:  Visit the official website: exams.nta.ac.in/ICAR.   Click on the link for “ICAR AIEEA PG 2025 / AICE Ph.D. 2025 Scorecard.”   Enter your Application Number and Date of Birth.   View your scorecard on the screen.   Download and print the scorecard for future reference.   Direct link to download the ICAR AIEEA PG 2025 and AICE PhD 2025 results is available here. Link 1, Link 2What's next for candidates? The AIEEA PG scores will be used for admissions to postgraduate courses in disciplines such as agriculture, horticulture, forestry, veterinary science, animal sciences, agricultural engineering, community science, fisheries, dairy science, and allied sciences. AICE Ph.D. scores will determine admission to doctoral programmes in corresponding fields.For further queries, candidates may contact the NTA Helpdesk at 011-40759000 or 69227700, or email icar@nta.ac.in.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
