The Punjab Police Constable recruitment process for 2025 has reached a crucial milestone with the release of the official scorecards on August 12, 2025. This recruitment drive is one of the largest in the state, attracting thousands of candidates aspiring to serve as police constables. The Computer-Based Test (CBT) was held recently, and candidates eagerly awaited their results to understand their performance and chances of moving forward in the selection process.Only those who clear the CBT with the minimum required marks will be allowed to move forward to the next stages of the recruitment process, such as physical fitness tests, document verification, and medical examinations.How to check Punjab Police Constable result 2025To view and download the scorecard, candidates should follow these simple steps:Visit the official Punjab Police recruitment website or the direct scorecard link: Punjab Police Constable Scorecard.Log in using your Registration Number or Login ID and Password.Access your scorecard, which details subject-wise marks, total marks obtained, and other relevant information.Download and save the scorecard for future reference.Direct link here.What does the scorecard indicate?The scorecard provides candidates with their marks in the Computer-Based Test (CBT), breaking down performance by subjects and showing the total score. This helps candidates assess their standing in the exam and estimate whether they qualify for the next recruitment stages such as Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and document verification.Next steps and selection processWhile the scorecards have been released, the final results, including the merit list and cut-off marks, are expected by the end of August 2025. Candidates are advised to monitor the official website regularly for these updates.
