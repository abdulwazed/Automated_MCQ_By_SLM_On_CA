In Delhi, two nine-year-old girls were allegedly gang-raped near a private swimming pool in Narela, with both accused now in custody.Bihar SIR: SC says 11 documents better than 7, not exclusionary without AadhaarThe Supreme Court observed that the 11 documents accepted for the Special Intensive Revision (SIR) in poll-bound Bihar make the process more "voter-friendly" than the seven documents required for a summary revision. Hearing pleas challenging EC's June 24 decision to hold the SIR without Aadhaar as valid ID, a bench of Justices Surya Kant and Joymalya Bagchi said that, contrary to claims of exclusion, the wider list appeared “actually inclusionary.” Read full storyJaishankar to visit Moscow for talks with Lavrov amid US-India trade tensionsExternal affairs minister S Jaishankar will visit Russia on August 21 to hold talks with his counterpart, Sergei Lavrov.
