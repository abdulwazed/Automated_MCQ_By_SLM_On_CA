War 2 is set to release on August 14, 2025. In a move that perfectly matches the high-octane energy of War 2, Hrithik Roshan’s fans in Mumbai are taking their celebration to an unprecedented level — by hosting a special 4 AM screening, billed as the 'First Show of India.' The early-morning extravaganza will not only mark the return of Kabir to the big screen but also honour Hrithik’s milestone 25 years in the film industry.IMAX Wadala booked for the eventAccording to a Pinkvilla report, Hrithik fans have coordinated with YRF and cinema halls to organise a special 4 AM screening of War 2 in Mumbai. The entire IMAX Wadala auditorium has been booked for the event, marking a first-of-its-kind format for the actor’s fanbase. Organisers recalled the electrifying atmosphere during War’s 2019 release and expressed excitement that this could be the first-ever show of War 2 in India, thanking YRF for their support in making it possible.Hrithik Roshan flaunts sculpted physique in unfiltered 'War 2' prep workout clipMoinnudin Rehmani told the news portal that action films featuring larger-than-life superstars are best enjoyed in the IMAX format. The special screening will take place at Miraj IMAX in Wadala, one of the largest IMAX screens in India, with a 4 AM show planned to celebrate their idol and give fans the thrill of being among the first to watch War 2.PollDo you think a 4 AM screening for a movie is a good idea?Yes, it adds excitement!No, too early for me.Fans mark Hrithik’s 25 years in cinemaKarthik Subramaniam further added that he considers the event special for multiple reasons, as it celebrates both War 2 and Hrithik Roshan’s 25 years in the film industry. Plans are underway to make the screening a unique experience, with the 4 AM timing marking a first for the fans. Another fan, Sneh Balwani, expressed excitement at simply watching Hrithik on the big screen, calling it a tradition to catch the first show of all his films — made even more remarkable this time with a 4 AM screening in Mumbai.War 2 hits theatres on August 14, 2025, and is expected to deliver strong box office results over the Independence Day weekend.About the AuthorTOI Entertainment DeskThe TOI Entertainment Desk is a dynamic and dedicated team of journalists, working tirelessly to bring the pulse of the entertainment world straight to the readers of The Times of India.
