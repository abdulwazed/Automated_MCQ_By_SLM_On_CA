Atif mourns father Muhammad Aslam’s passing
Pakistani singer Atif Aslam's father, Muhammad Aslam, passed away at 77 after battling health issues following a heart attack. Atif shared an emotional tribute to his 'Iron Man' on Instagram, prompting condolences from fans and celebrities worldwide. The funeral took place in Lahore, with many remembering Atif's father and legacy.

The music industry and millions of fans were left in mourning on Tuesday, August 13, as Pakistani singing sensation Atif Aslam announced the death of his father, Muhammad Aslam, at the age of 77. Months after surviving a heart attack, the man Atif affectionately called his “Iron Man” passed away following ongoing health struggles.'A final good bye to my Iron Man'In an emotional Instagram post, Atif shared a tender photograph with his father and wrote, “A final good bye to my Iron Man. Rest in love Abu g.” Although his social media remains inaccessible in India, screenshots of the tribute quickly spread online, touching fans across borders. The final rites are set to take place in Valencia Town, Lahore, after Asr prayers.Check out the post here.An outpouring of supportThe news sparked a wave of condolences from celebrities, fellow musicians, and fans worldwide. Punjab Chief Minister Maryam Nawaz Sharif extended her sympathies, praying for the family’s strength during this time. One fan tweeted, "Life is so unpredictable. Yesterday we watched Atif Aslam light up the stage, and today we hear the heartbreaking news of his father’s passing. Behind the music, behind the applause, performers carry silent battles we never see. May Allah grant his father the highest place in Jannah and give Atif Aslam & family strength in this difficult time."A legacy beyond bordersAtif Aslam, adored in both Pakistan and India, is known for delivering timeless songs such as 'Tera Hone Laga Hoon,' 'Jeene Laga Hoon,' and 'Pehli Nazar Mein.', and many more as we all know and cherish.
