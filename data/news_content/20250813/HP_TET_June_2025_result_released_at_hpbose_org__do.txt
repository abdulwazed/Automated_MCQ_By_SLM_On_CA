HP TET June 2025 result: The Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education (HPBOSE) has declared the HP TET June 2025 results on August 13, 2025. A total of 31,506 candidates appeared for the exams held across five dates in ten subjects. Out of these, 10,880 candidates qualified. The final answer key has also been released. Results are available on the official website, and certificates can be accessed via Digi Locker. Candidates can check their results using their Roll No or Application No.

The examination was conducted across the state in both morning and evening sessions and covered a total of ten subjects.According to HPBOSE, the final results were released on August 13, 2025, and are now available on the board’s official website, www.hpbose.org. Candidates can check their individual results by entering their ROLL NO or APPLICATION NO through the "TET JUNE-2025 LINK" provided on the homepage.Exams conducted across multiple dates and subjectsThe HP TET June 2025 examinations were held on June 1, June 8, June 11, June 14, and July 12, 2025. Subjects included TGT (Arts), TGT (Medical), TGT (Non-Medical), TGT (Hindi), TGT (Sanskrit), JBT, Punjabi, Urdu, Special Educator TET (For Pre-Primary to Class V), and Special Educator TET (For Class VI to Class XII).A total of 34,599 candidates applied for the examination. Of these, 31,506 candidates appeared, while 3,093 remained absent. No unfair means cases (UMC) were reported.
