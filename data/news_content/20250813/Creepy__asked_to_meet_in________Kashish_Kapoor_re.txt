'Creepy, asked to meet in ... ': Kashish Kapoor reveals flirty cricketer made her uncomfortable, refuses to name him
Kashish Kapoor, the actress and reality TV figure, revealed that a famous cricketer made her uncomfortable by persistently asking to meet her in person. The incident, shared in an interview on social media, has sparked considerable speculation among fans regarding the cricketer's identity, especially after her appearance on Bigg Boss 18.
