SHS Bihar ANM recruitment 2025: Apply online for 5006 vacancies at shs.bihar.gov.in before 28 August
Bihar ANM recruitment 2025: The State Health Society Bihar (SHS Bihar) has announced 5006 vacancies for Auxiliary Nurse Midwife (ANM) posts. Eligible candidates with a recognised ANM diploma can apply online at shs.bihar.gov.in from August 14 to August 28, 2025. The recruitment includes ANM (HSC), ANM (RBSK), and ANM (NUHM) posts. Detailed eligibility, age limits, and application fees are specified. The monthly salary is Rs 15,000. Candidates must register with the Bihar Nurses Registration Council to apply.

Bihar ANM vacancy 2025: Eligibility, application fee and online application details Bihar ANM recruitment 2025: The State Health Society Bihar (SHS Bihar) has announced a recruitment drive for 5006 Auxiliary Nurse Midwife (ANM) vacancies. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online at the official website shs.bihar.gov.in. The application process opens at 10:00 AM on August 14, 2025, and the last date to submit the application form is 6:00 PM on August 28, 2025.This recruitment drive will fill positions across three categories: 4197 ANM (HSC) posts, 510 ANM (RBSK) posts, and 299 ANM (NUHM) posts. Candidates who meet the eligibility criteria, including educational qualifications and age limits, are encouraged to apply within the stipulated timeline to secure a position.Vacancy details and eligibility criteriaThe total number of vacancies for the SHS Bihar ANM recruitment 2025 stands at 5006. The breakdown is as follows:S. No.ANM categoryNumber of posts1ANM (HSC)4,1972ANM (RBSK)5103ANM (NUHM)299Total5,006Candidates must have completed a two-year full-time ANM training course (diploma) from a recognised institution. Additionally, registration with the Bihar Nurses Registration Council is mandatory. The minimum age to apply is 21 years as of August 1, 2025.The maximum age limits for female candidates are specified by category:• Unreserved/EWS: 40 years• Backward/Extremely Backward Class: 40 years• Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe: 42 yearsAge relaxation will be provided as per government rules.Application fee detailsThe application fee for the SHS Bihar ANM recruitment varies based on category and domicile status:S. No.CategoryApplication fee (Rs)1General/Backward/Extremely Backward Class/EWS5002Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe permanent residents of Bihar1253Reserved/Unreserved female candidates from Bihar1254All candidates from outside Bihar (male/female)5005Divyang candidates with 40% or more disability125How to apply online for SHS Bihar ANM recruitment 2025Candidates can follow these five steps to apply online:Step 1: Visit the official website: shs.bihar.gov.inStep 2: Locate the SHS Bihar ANM recruitment 2025 notification and read it carefully.Step 3: Register on the portal using a valid email ID and mobile number.Step 4: Fill in the online application form with all required personal, educational, and contact details.Step 5: Upload scanned copies of the necessary documents and pay the application fee as per category. Submit the form before the deadline.Candidates are advised to review all details thoroughly before submitting the application to avoid errors.Direct link to download the official Bihar ANM recruitment 2025 notificationClick here to visit the official website Salary and additional informationThe selected candidates for the ANM posts will receive a monthly salary of Rs 15,000. The recruitment notification also provides information about the selection process and document verification, which candidates can find in the official PDF notification released on August 12, 2025, available on the SHS Bihar website.The recruitment drive aims to fill vital healthcare positions across the state, strengthening Bihar’s public health infrastructure through the appointment of qualified Auxiliary Nurse Midwives.All interested candidates should ensure they apply online before 6:00 PM on August 28, 2025, to participate in the recruitment process.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
