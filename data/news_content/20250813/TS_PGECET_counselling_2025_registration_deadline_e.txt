The Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE) has extended the registration deadline for the TS PGECET 2025 Phase I counselling to August 18, 2025. This extension provides candidates additional time to complete the registration process for admission into M.Tech, M.Pharm, M.Arch, and PharmD programs offered by various institutions in Telangana. The revised schedule also includes updated dates for certificate verification, web options entry, and seat allotment. Candidates are advised to adhere to the new timelines to ensure their participation in the counselling process.Eligibility criteria for TS PGECETCandidates are eligible for the counselling process if they have:Qualified TS PGECET 2025, orA valid GATE/GPAT score from 2023, 2024, or 2025.Please note that seats will be filled primarily based on GATE and GPAT scores; TS PGECET candidates will be considered only for remaining seats.How to register for TS PGECETHere is how candidates can register for the counselling process:Visit the official counselling portal: pgecetadm.tgche.ac.inClick on 'TS PGECET Phase I Counselling Registration'Login using your hall ticket number and PGECET rank or GATE/GPAT scoreFill out the registration form and upload required documentsPay the processing feeSubmit the form and take a printout for future referenceImportant documents requiredParticipating candidates have to submit the following documents for verification process:TS PGECET Rank Card or GATE/GPAT Score CardDegree Certificate and MarksheetsIntermediate (10+2) Certificate and MarksheetsCaste Certificate (if applicable)Special Category Certificates (NCC, CAP, PH, Sports)Income Certificate (if applicable)Aadhar CardPassport-sized PhotographsTOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
