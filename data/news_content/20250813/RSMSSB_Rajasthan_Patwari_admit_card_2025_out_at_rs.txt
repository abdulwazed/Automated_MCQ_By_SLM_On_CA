Rajasthan Patwari admit card 2025 out, RSMSSB to conduct exam for 3,705 vacancies on August 17 The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has officially released the RSMSSB Rajasthan Patwari Admit Card 2025. Candidates who are set to appear for the Rajasthan Patwari Exam 2025 can now download their admit cards from the official recruitment portal at recruitment.rajasthan.gov.in.According to the official notice, the admit card link was activated on August 13, 2025. The Rajasthan Patwari Exam 2025 is scheduled to be conducted on August 17, 2025, in two shifts. Candidates must carry a printed copy of the admit card to the examination centre along with a valid photo ID.RSMSSB to conduct Patwari recruitment exam for 3,705 vacanciesThe RSMSSB will conduct the Rajasthan Patwari Exam 2025 to fill a total of 3,705 vacancies, which include 3,183 non-TSP and 522 TSP posts. The examination will be held in offline (written) mode and divided into two shifts. Shift 1 will be held from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM, and Shift 2 will be from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM. All candidates are advised to report to their designated centres at least two hours before the scheduled exam time for proper verification and security checks.Admit card is mandatory for entry to exam hallThe RSMSSB Rajasthan Patwari Admit Card 2025 is an essential document for candidates appearing in the exam. It contains critical information, including the candidate's name, roll number, registration number, photograph, signature, shift timing, exam centre address, and exam-related instructions. Candidates are advised to carefully check all details printed on the admit card for accuracy.Official websites to download the admit cardCandidates can download their RSMSSB Patwari Admit Card 2025 by logging in to either rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in or sso.rajasthan.gov.in/signin using their SSO ID and password. The admit card must be downloaded and printed, and at least two to three copies should be kept for future use.How to download RSMSSB Rajasthan Patwari Admit Card 2025Follow the steps below to download the admit card:Step 1: Visit the official website at rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in or the SSO portal at sso.rajasthan.gov.in/signin.Step 2: On the homepage, click on the "Admit Card" or "Recruitment" section.Step 3: Find the link titled "Rajasthan Patwari Admit Card 2025" and click on it.Step 4: Enter your Application Number and Date of Birth or login with your SSO ID and password.Step 5: After submitting the details, the admit card will be displayed. Download it and take multiple printouts for reference.Direct link to download the RSMSSB Rajasthan Patwari admit card 2025Details mentioned on the Rajasthan Patwari Admit Card 2025Candidates should verify the following details on the admit card:• Candidate's name• Roll number• Registration number• Photograph and signature• Exam date and shift timing• Examination centre name and address• Important instructions for the examExam day guidelines issued by RSSBCandidates must adhere to all instructions provided on the admit card. Entry to the examination hall will not be allowed without a valid admit card and a government-issued photo ID. All candidates are required to reach the exam venue two hours prior to the exam to complete the verification process.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
