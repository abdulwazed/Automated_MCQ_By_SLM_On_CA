Buckle up — the drama is delicious!War 2 FIRST reviews out: Fans call it a 'sure-shot hit'Early reviews of War 2, starring Hrithik Roshan, Jr NTR, and Kiara Advani, are lighting up social media ahead of its August 14 release. Critics are hailing its high-energy action and standout chemistry between the leads. Despite some criticism of the plot and visual effects, expectations for box-office success this Independence Day weekend remain strong. Hrithik Roshan flaunts sculpted physique in unfiltered 'War 2' prep workout clipAkshay's car gets seized in Jammu for tinted windowAkshay Kumar's car was seized by Jammu traffic police for violating legal restrictions on tinted windows during a routine inspection. The vehicle's darkened windows exceeded permissible limits under the Motor Vehicles Act. Authorities emphasized that traffic laws apply equally to all individuals, regardless of status. Despite the impoundment, Kumar's scheduled event proceeded as planned with alternative transportation arranged. Boney Kapoor celebrates wife Sridevi's 62nd birth anniversaryOn August 13, 2025, Boney Kapoor commemorated his late wife Sridevi’s 62nd birth anniversary with a heartfelt tribute on social media. He fondly recalled how her "few words in broken Hindi and English" deeply moved him and sparked his curiosity to know her better. Their love story began on the sets of Mr. India in 1987, where Boney went out of his way to make Sridevi feel special. Despite being married to Mona Shourie Kapoor at the time, Boney confessed his feelings to Sridevi, leading to a deep and lasting bond. Their relationship, though controversial, blossomed into a loving marriage and a family legacy.Armaan Malik and his wives summoned by courtYouTuber Armaan Malik and his two wives, Payal and Kritika, have been summoned by a Patiala district court on September 2, 2025. The summons stem from allegations of bigamy and offending religious sentiments. A petition claims Armaan has four marriages, violating the Hindu Marriage Act, and that Payal's Instagram video dressed as Hindu goddess Kali insulted religious beliefs. In response, the couple visited temples and performed penance rituals to seek forgiveness. Mrunal drops post after backlash over calling Bipasha 'manly'Mrunal Thakur recently addressed online backlash stemming from a resurfaced clip where she referred to Bipasha Basu as "manly." In response, she shared bold Instagram photos with the caption "Stop staring!" Additionally, rumors about a relationship with actor Dhanush were circulating, which she clarified, stating he is "just a good friend" and that his presence at her film's premiere was due to an invitation from Ajay Devgn.
