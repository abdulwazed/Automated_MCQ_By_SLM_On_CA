Carry it with a valid photo ID. Pre-Exam Training admit cards are already out.  The IBPS PO Preliminary Exam admit card 2025 is expected to be released soon on the official website ibps.in. The prelims exam dates are August 17, 23, and 24, 2025. Candidates need to download the admit card using their registration number and password or date of birth. The admit card contains exam details such as date, time, and exam center. It is mandatory to carry the admit card and a valid photo ID to the exam center. Pre-Examination Training admit cards for SC/ST/OBC/Minority candidates were released on August 11, 2025. The mains exam is scheduled for October 12, 2025.How to download the IBPS PO prelims admit card?The admit card for IBPS PO preliminary exam is expected to be released any time soon. Here’s how you can download your hall ticket easily, once released:Go to the official website: ibps.in.Find the section called CRP-PO/MT-XV (this is the current recruitment).Click on the link that says IBPS PO Prelims Admit Card 2025.Enter your registration number or roll number and password or date of birth.Submit the details to see your admit card.Download it and take a printout to carry on exam day.What information is on the admit card?After downloading the IBPS PO admit card, registered candidates are advised to cross-check all the details mentioned in it carefully. Your admit card will have:Your name and roll numberDate, time, and shift of the examAddress of the exam centerImportant exam instructionsYour photograph and signatureWhat to carry on exam dayTo ensure smooth entry and avoid any issues at the exam center, candidates must bring:Printed Admit Card: A clear, printed copy of your IBPS PO prelims admit card.Valid Photo Identification: One government-issued ID such as:Aadhaar cardPAN cardPassportPassport-Size Photographs: If specifically mentioned in your admit card instructions, carry recent passport-size photos.Note: Without these documents, candidates will not be permitted to enter the examination hall.
