There's a new photo of Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, and it is taking social media by storm.Ash, who made waves early this week for her motivating post for a global beauty brand, hit the social media trends once again when a stunning photo from the campaign shoot turned up online. The actress' makeup artist, Ardian Jacobs, took to Instagram to share the stunning click online. The stunning click gave fans a closer look at Ash's boss-lady look. For the shoot, she donned a crisp white shirt and topped it off with what seemed to be cape coat. Shrima Rai Recreates Aishwarya Rai’s Cannes Look—Months after Clout DenialThe new picThe latest photo is reminiscent of her powerful turn as a lawyer in the 2015 film Jazbaa. In the Sanjay Gupta-directed thriller, the actress played the role of a criminal lawyer who juggled her high-stakes courtroom battle of defending a criminal, all while trying to save her kidnapped daughter.Ash's new ad shootIn the video from the ad shoot, Ash shared a rather empowering message for all her fans. She says, "There’s not much difference between social media and social pressure. As a woman and a mother, it’s a matter of grave concern to think about the power it can have over young minds. Frankly, even impressionable adults."Recent spottingThe actress, who has been keeping a relatively low profile in recent months, continues to stay in the spotlight through her occasional public appearances. She was most recently spotted at the Mumbai airport, on her return from her holiday with hubby Abhishek Bachchan and daughter Aaradhya Bachchan.
