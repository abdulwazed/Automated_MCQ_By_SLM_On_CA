The results of the NEET-PG 2025 exam were announced by the National Board of Examinations in Medical Sciences (NBEMS) on August 19. The postgraduate medical entrance exam was held on August 3 in one shift across the country. This year, more than 2.42 lakh candidates appeared for the exam, which decides admission to MD, MS and PG Diploma seats in government and private colleges. Students can check their results on the official websites, natboard.edu.in and nbe.edu.in. NBEMS has also released the category-wise cut-offs. The individual scorecards will be available for download from August 29. Next, candidates will have to take part in counselling for seat allotment.NEET PG 2025 cut off marksNBEMS has also released category wise cut off and percentile scores for candidates along with the results. These scorecards will only be valid for the 2025–26 admission cycle.
