Prabhas and Anushka Shetty to reunite after 8 years
Prabhas and Anushka Shetty, celebrated for their iconic roles in 'Baahubali,' are reportedly reuniting for a special interview after nearly eight years, commemorating the tenth anniversary of 'Baahubali: The Beginning.' The duo is expected to share insights about their journey and experiences within the franchise, though official confirmation and details of the interview are awaited by eager fans.

Picture Credit: Facebook Amarendra Baahubali and Devasena are among the most iconic character pairings in recent film history, and portraying them by Prabhas and Anushka's onscreen pairing remains one of the most loved by the fans. Their natural chemistry in both 'Baahubali: The Beginning' and its sequel, 'Baahubali: The Conclusion', gained praise among the fans. Latest reports suggest the co-stars are reportedly reuniting after nearly eight years.According to a Pinkvilla report, the duo is set to come together for a special interview that will soon hit digital platforms. However, it is imperative to add that there has been no official confirmation yet.Prabhas Fans Go Wild at ‘The RajaSaab’ Teaser Launch in Hyderabad | Milk Bath, Dhol & Cutout Maniacelebrating a decade of 'Baahubali'The reunion is said to be related to the milestone of 'Baahubali: The Beginning,' which recently celebrated its tenth release anniversary. The film, directed by visionary filmmaker SS Rajamouli, not only shattered box-office records but also became a cultural phenomenon. It is said that Prabhas and Anushka will talk about their journey, experiences, and legacy in the franchise.Although the specifics of the format and release date of the reported interview being filmed are not yet specified, fans are waiting for official word. Just the possibility of seeing Prabhas and Anushka together again, even if it's not in a film but in a casual conversation, has kept social media talking.about 'Baahubali'The 'Baahubali' franchise is set in the fictional kingdom of Mahishmati and follows the epic saga of two brothers, Amarendra Baahubali and Bhallaladeva, who are locked in a fierce struggle for the throne.their upcoming filmsOn the current work front, Prabhas is working on multiple projects. He will be next seen in the horror-comedy film 'The Raja Saab'. He is working on a war drama film with director Hanu Raghavapudi and a cop action drama, 'Spirit', directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga.On the other hand, Anushka Shetty will be seen in a fierce role in the upcoming film, 'Ghaati'.
