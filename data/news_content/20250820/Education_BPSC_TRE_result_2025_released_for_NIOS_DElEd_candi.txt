The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has declared the results of the Teacher Recruitment Examination (TRE) 2025 for candidates who completed the 18-month Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed) course from the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS). The decision follows a prolonged legal battle and a court directive, bringing major relief to thousands of aspirants.According to the commission, results for three primary-level subjects and six middle-level subjects have been released. A subject-wise list of eligible candidates has also been made available on the official website — bpsc.bihar.gov.inHow to check BPSC TRE result 2025Candidates can access their results online by following these steps:Visit the official website bpsc.bihar.gov.in.  On the homepage, locate the School Teacher Competitive Examination, TRE 2.0 section.  The option to download subject-wise PDF lists will be available there.  Click on the link and download the list for future reference.Direct link to download BPSC TRE result 2025 is available here. BPSC TRE result 2025: Subject-wise performanceThis year, a total of 389 candidates have cleared the examination across subjects. These include:Hindi – 100 candidatesSanskrit – 65 candidatesEnglish – 243 candidatesSocial Science – 233 candidatesMathematics – 249 candidatesUrdu – 171 candidatesWhat's next? The Bihar Public Service Commission had conducted the recruitment examination to fill approximately 1.22 lakh teaching posts across the state. The written examination was held between 7 December and 15 December 2023, with nearly 7 lakh aspirants appearing.With the declaration of results, a wave of relief has spread among candidates who had been awaiting clarity for months.
