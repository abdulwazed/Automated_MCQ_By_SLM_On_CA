Hollywood's BFFs Jennifer Aniston and Courteney Cox reunited for a casual day out with their partners and photos from their double date are fuelling speculations. The 'Friends' stars were spotted with their partners at Nobu in Malibu. While Aniston was accompanied by her hypnotist boyfriend Jim Curtis, Cox was joined by longtime partner and Snow Patrol musician Johnny McDaid. Dinner datePaparazzi spotted the couple on their exit from the restaurant after dinner. The spotting comes amidst ongoing buzz about Aniston’s blossoming romance with her beau, with reports stating that the actress may just be ready to give marriage another shot.Wedding buzzAccording to Radar Online, Aniston and Curtis, who have known each other for years but only started dating a few months ago, are reportedly ready to go all in and tie the knot. The report states that the two are “madly in love” and sees Curtis as her “forever guy” after two failed marriages. “Jen and Jim are crazy about each other, and she’s at a stage in life that’s tired of the dating playing field. She’s going all in on this romance,” an insider told the portal.Wedding planningThe couple is said to have already discussed their future together and according to the report, are even planning their dreamy beach wedding. It goes on to state that Aniston is reportedly considering an intimate ceremony in Cabo, surrounded by close friends.The Emmy-winning actress was first linked to Curtis last month, after the pair were spotted packing on the PDA while holidaying in Spain. They were even seen spending time in New York with the actress' friends Jason Bateman and Amanda Anka.While neither Aniston nor Curtis has publicly addressed their blossoming romance. If rumours of a marriage on the cards is true, then this will be heer third time down the aisle. She was first married to Brad Pitt from 2000 to 2005.
