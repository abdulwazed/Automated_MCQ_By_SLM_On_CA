Dhanashree Verma breaks silence on divorce; calls out Yuzvendra Chahal
Dhanashree Verma has addressed her divorce from Yuzvendra Chahal, expressing her sadness and criticising his choice of attire at the hearing. Chahal's t-shirt, bearing the words “Be your own sugar daddy”, sparked controversy and social media backlash. Dhanashree revealed her emotional breakdown during the verdict and how the incident ultimately motivated her to move on.
