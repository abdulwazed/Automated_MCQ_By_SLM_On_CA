Jessica Hines who is a UK based author and journalist has been in the news lately for her relationship rumours with Aamir Khan. These rumours were rife for many years now but recently, Aamir's brother Faissal Khan has claimed that Aamir was in a relationship with Jessica and they have an illegitimate child named Jaan, together out of wedlock. Amidst Faisal's recent comments, Jessica's old interview has gone viral. For those not in the know, Jessica is the author of the book, 'Big B: Bollywood Bachchan and Me'. She wrote extensively about Indian cinema and the actors here in the 1990s and early 2000s. As per reports, Aamir and Jessica got close to each other on the sets of 'Ghulaam'. While neither Aamir nor Jessica have publicly addressed their relationship, reportedly Jessica was raising her son Jaan in London. Now amidst Faissal's recent comment, Jessica's old interview has gone viral. During a chat with News18 Showsha, when she was asked about her bonding with the 'Lagaan' actor, she said, "Let's not comment about that." Faissal has broken all ties with Aamir and his family and claimed that they called him mad, tortured him, Aamir also locked him at his house. He said that his family began to treat him badly after they were pressurising him to marry his aunty (His mother's cousin) and he refused. He had then spoken about Aamir's marriages and affair. He had said at the press conference on August 18, "Aamir was married, got divorced from Reena, and then he had a relationship with Jessica Hines, with whom he also has an illegitimate child, out of wedlock."PollDo you believe the rumors about Aamir Khan and Jessica Hines having a child together?Yes, I believe the rumors.No, I don't believe the rumors.On work front, Aamir was last seen in 'Sitaare Zameen Par'.
