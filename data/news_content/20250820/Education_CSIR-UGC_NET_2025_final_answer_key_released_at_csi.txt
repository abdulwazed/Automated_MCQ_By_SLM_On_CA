The National Testing Agency (NTA) has released the final answer key for the CSIR-UGC NET June 2025 session on its official website csirnet.nta.ac.in. This marks a crucial step for thousands of candidates who appeared for the examination on July 28, 2025, across India. The final key comes after the provisional answer key and the objection window, which allows candidates to challenge any discrepancies.Notably, the NTA has dropped one question from the Life Sciences paper, and candidates who attempted it will receive full marks. With the final answer key out, the results are expected shortly, allowing students to check their qualifying status and prepare for further research opportunities in their respective science streams.How to download the CSIR NET final answer keyStudents who appeared for the CSIR NET examination can download the final answer key by following the steps below: Visit csirnet.nta.ac.inClick on the “Final Answer Key 2025” linkEnter your application number, date of birth, and security pinDownload the PDF and verify your responsesDirect link to download CSIR NET final answer key 2025 here.Minimum qualifying marksAs per the NTA guidelines, candidates have to obtain the following minimum marks in order to qualify in the CSIR NET examination:CategoryMinimum Marks in Each PartGeneral / EWS / OBC33%SC / ST / PwD25%Note: No separate cut-off exists for Parts A, B, and CWhat’s next?Candidates can check their CSIR-UGC NET 2025 results using their login credentials, including their application number, date of birth, and security pin. The results are expected to be declared shortly after the release of the final answer key.
