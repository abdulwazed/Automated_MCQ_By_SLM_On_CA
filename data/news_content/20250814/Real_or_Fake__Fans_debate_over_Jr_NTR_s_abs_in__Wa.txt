'War 2' hit theatres today and fans who rushed for the early screenings had a lot to say. One hot topic of discussion among fans, was lead actor Jr NTR’s chiselled 8-pack abs in his opening scene. Social media has been abuzz with fans debating over whether the actor's ripped physique is the result of intense training or heavy-handed VFX.Several admirers of the star took to their Twitter handles to share photos of the actor flexing his abs in a shirtless scene and praise the actor’s transformation. One fan wrote, "#JrNTR's cinematic journey has been undoubtedly inspirational..."However, not everyone is convinced. A section of viewers claimed the abs looked “artificial,” sparking speculation about the use of CGI. Critics even described the scene as “Worst ever VFX", arguing it took away from the realism of the action sequences. One asked, "If this is really in the movie then they have spoiled the original aura of #JrNTR. Can anyone confirm? Why to show fake abs always??"Still another said, "Worst. VFX. Ever.
