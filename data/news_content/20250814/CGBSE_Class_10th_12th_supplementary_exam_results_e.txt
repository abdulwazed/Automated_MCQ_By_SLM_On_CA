The Chhattisgarh Board of Secondary Education (CGBSE) is expected to release the supplementary examination results for Class 10th and Class 12th soon on its official websites: cgbse.nic.in and results.cg.nic.in. Students will be able to check and download their mark sheets for the CGBSE 10th and 12th supplementary examinations 2025 by logging in with their credentials, including roll numbers. The CGBSE Class 10th supplementary examinations for 2025 were held from July 9 to July 21, while the Class 12th supplementary examinations were conducted from July 8 to July 22. Once the results are announced, Class 10th students will be required to select their academic stream — humanities, commerce, or science, based on their interests. Class 12th students can choose from a range of undergraduate degree programmes according to their preferred field of study and career aspirations.How to check CGBSE 10th, 12th supplementary results 2025 Candidates can follow these steps to download their scorecards once released:  Visit the official website: cgbse.nic.in.   Navigate to the "Student Corner" section on the homepage.   Select the “High School Second Main Supplementary 2025” or “Higher Secondary Second Main Supplementary 2025” link, depending on your class.   Enter your roll number and the given captcha code.   View your CGBSE 10th or 12th supplementary results displayed on the screen.   Download and take a printout of the scorecard for future reference.
