Mass shooting in US: Several law enforcement officials shot in southern Virginia; what we know so far
A shooting incident occurred in Pittsylvania County, Virginia. Several law enforcement officers sustained injuries. Representative John McGuire expressed support for the injured deputies and their families. He mentioned that he is closely monitoring the situation. Pittsylvania County is located near the border of North Carolina. Further details are awaited as the situation unfolds.

Taylor.Images and footage from the scene depicted a significant presence of police and emergency response vehicles stationed in the vicinity.Reports of deputies injured Virginia shootingThe incident led to a standoff that lasted several hours, but it concluded peacefully with the suspect being taken into custody, Sheriff Taylor reported to the media.
