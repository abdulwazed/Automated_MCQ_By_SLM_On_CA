The Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) has officially released the admit card for the State Engineering Service (SES) Exam 2025. Scheduled to be held on August 24, 2025, the SES exam is a crucial opportunity for engineering graduates aspiring to secure government positions in Madhya Pradesh. Candidates who have successfully registered for the exam can now download their hall tickets from the official MPPSC website. The admit card serves as a mandatory document for exam entry and carries important details including roll number, exam center, timings, and guidelines. With the examination approaching, candidates are advised to check all details carefully, ensure they meet the reporting requirements, and prepare for the test in line with official instructions.How to download the MPPSC SES admit card 2025Here is how candidates can download their MPPSC SES hall tickets online:Visit the official MPPSC website: mppsc.gov.in.Click on the link labeled “State Engineering Service Exam 2025 Admit Card.”Enter your application number and date of birth.Submit the details to access your hall ticket.Download and print the admit card for exam day.Direct link to download MPPSC admit card.Details mentioned on the admit cardAfter downloading the MPPSC admit card, candidates are advised to check all the details mentioned on it carefully. The following information will be mentioned in the hall ticket:Candidate’s name and photographRoll number and shift detailsExam date, time, and reporting timeExam center addressInstructions to follow on the exam dayExam day guidelinesCandidates appearing for the MPPSC SES exam are advised to follow the instructions provided below on the day of examination. Carry a printed copy of the admit card.Bring a valid photo ID (Aadhar, voter ID, passport, or driving license).Reach the exam center at least 30 minutes early.Follow all instructions mentioned in the admit card.For more updates and information, candidates can visit the official MPPSC website: mppsc.gov.in.Ready to navigate global policies?
