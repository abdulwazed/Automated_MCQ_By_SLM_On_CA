The State Bank of India (SBI) is expected to release the Probationary Officer (PO) Preliminary Exam 2025 results very soon. The Prelims exam was conducted across India on 2nd, 4th, and 5th August 2025, with thousands of candidates appearing at various centers. The result will be available on the official SBI website, sbi.co.in.SBI has not announced the exact date of result declaration. However, based on past trends, results are usually released within a few weeks of the exam. Candidates are advised to regularly check the SBI Careers page for official updates to avoid missing any announcements. Only candidates who qualify the Prelims will move to the Main Examination, which is the next stage in the recruitment process for SBI PO positions.How to check and download SBI PO resultOnce the result is released, candidates can follow these steps:Visit the SBI Careers PortalGo to the Recruitment Results page on the official SBI website.Look for “Probationary Officers (Advertisement No: CRPD/PO/2024-25/22)” under the Prelims section.Fill in your Roll Number/Registration Number and Date of Birth accurately.Click Submit to see your result.Save a digital copy and take a printout for future reference, especially for Main exam registration.Details mentioned in SBI PO prelims scorecardAfter downloading the scorecards, candidates are advised to check all the details carefully. The following details will be mentioned in the SBI PO scorecards:Candidate Info: Name, Roll Number/Registration Number, Date of Birth, CategoryExam Info: Exam name, date, shift/sessionSection-wise Marks: English, Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning AbilityTotal Marks: Marks obtained and maximum marksQualifying Status: Whether you cleared Prelims and eligible for Main ExamCut-off Marks: Category-wise section and overall cut-offsInstructions: Notes about Main Exam and preserving the scorecardWhat happens after the prelims?Candidates who qualify the Prelims are eligible to appear for the Main Exam, which is the next critical stage in SBI PO recruitment. The marks obtained in Prelims are only qualifying and do not count toward the final selection. The Main Exam, followed by the Group Exercise and Interview, determines the final selection of candidates.Ready to navigate global policies? Secure your overseas future.
