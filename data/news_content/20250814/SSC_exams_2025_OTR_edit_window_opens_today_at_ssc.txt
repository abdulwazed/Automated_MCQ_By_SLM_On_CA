The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has announced that candidates can now edit their One Time Registration (OTR) profiles from today, August 14, until August 31, 2025. This facility is available for candidates who have already created OTR profiles and wish to update or correct their details ahead of upcoming recruitment examinations. Candidates with queries can reach out to the SSC help desk via telephone or email. The commission has clarified that requests submitted in physical form will not be accepted.What is OTR in SSC recruitment? One Time Registration (OTR) is a unified registration process that allows candidates to apply for multiple SSC recruitment examinations using a single account. By completing the OTR process, candidates can avoid registering separately for each exam. Creating an OTR profile involves the following steps:  Personal details: Enter candidate name, identification, and contact information   Password creation: Set a new login password   Additional details: Provide nationality, address, and educational qualifications   Declaration: Confirm all information submittedHow to make changes online Candidates can follow these steps to update their OTR profiles online:  Visit the official SSC website: ssc.gov.in   Log in using your OTR credentials.   Go to the ‘Edit OTR’ section.   Make the necessary corrections and save your changes.   Verify all information before final submission.  Note: Candidates are advised to ensure that all details are accurate before submission, as these will be used for future SSC examinations.SSC CGL exam 2025 postponedThe commission has also postponed the Combined Graduate Level Examination (CGLE) 2025, originally scheduled to begin on August 13, 2025. SSC stated that it will conduct a thorough review of the examination platform and operational readiness. The CGL exam is now expected to start in the first week of September 2025, and a revised schedule will be released on the official website.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
