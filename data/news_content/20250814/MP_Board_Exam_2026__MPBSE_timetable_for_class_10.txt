The Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (MPBSE) has officially released the examination timetable for the 2026 academic session for Class 10 and Class 12 students. With over 15 lakh students expected to appear, the timetable provides clarity on exam dates, subjects, and practical schedules, enabling better preparation and planning. All theory examinations will be conducted in the morning session from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM, while practical exams will be held in respective schools from February 10 to March 10, 2026.
