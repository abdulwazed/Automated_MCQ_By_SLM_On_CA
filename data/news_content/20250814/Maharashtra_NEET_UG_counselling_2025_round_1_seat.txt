The Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test (CET) Cell has officially announced the Round 1 seat allotment result for NEET UG 2025. This list is valid for admissions under the Centralised Admission Process (CAP) for MBBS and BDS courses in government, aided, and private medical colleges across Maharashtra.Candidates who participated in the counselling process can now access their allotment status and download the seat allotment letter from the official website: cetcell.mahacet.org. The reporting period for allotted candidates is from August 14 to August 22, 2025, excluding August 15 and 16. It's imperative for candidates to adhere to these dates to confirm their admission and proceed with the necessary documentation.How to check the round 1 seat allotment resultTo view and download the seat allotment letter, candidates should follow these steps:Visit the official website: cetcell.mahacet.org.Navigate to the "NEET UG Counselling 2025" section.Click on the "Round 1 Seat Allotment Result" link.Enter your login credentials (Application Number and Password).View and download the seat allotment letter for future reference.Direct link here.What to do after seat allotmentCandidates who have been allotted a seat are required to report to the respective colleges between August 14 and August 22, 2025 (excluding August 15 and 16). During this period, candidates must complete the following:Document Verification: Submit original documents for verification.Fee Payment: Pay the required admission fees.Status Retention: Fill out and submit the Status Retention Form.Failure to report within the specified timeframe may result in the cancellation of the allotted seat.Guidelines for candidatesCandidates should ensure that all details in the seat allotment letter, such as name, category, and allotted college, are accurate.In case of any discrepancies or grievances, candidates must contact the CET Cell immediately for resolution.Stay updated with any further announcements or instructions by regularly checking the official website.This seat allotment is the second to final step in the NEET UG 2025 admission process, and candidates are advised to follow all instructions diligently to secure their admission.Ready to navigate global policies?
