The State Bank of India (SBI) has released the first waiting list for the recruitment of Junior Associates (Customer Support and Sales) against non-joining and resignation cases. Candidates who were placed on the waitlist can now check their selection status through the SBI Clerk waiting list 2025, which includes the roll numbers of provisionally shortlisted candidates, on the official website: sbi.co.in. SBI has clarified that the selection of candidates will be provisional and subject to qualifying the proficiency test in the local language, meeting all eligibility criteria, and verifying the authenticity of information provided during registration. In addition, the bank has also announced a separate waitlist for the special recruitment drive for the Union Territory of Ladakh, covering Leh and Kargil Valley. A total of 13 candidates have been shortlisted under this category for further procedure. Candidates became eligible for selection in the waitlist after initially selected candidates either did not join or resigned, resulting in vacant positions.How to download SBI Clerk waiting list 2025Candidates can follow the steps below to download the SBI Clerk waiting list 2025 PDF from the official website.  Visit the official website: sbi.co.in   Navigate to the Careers section.   Select the Recruitment Results tab.   Click on the link titled “Waiting List of Provisionally Selected Candidates”.   The PDF will appear on the screen.   Download and save it for future reference.   Direct link to download SBI Clerk waiting list 2025 is available here: Link 1, Link 2SBI Clerk Main examination 2025 overview The Main examination was conducted on April 10 and April 12 for candidates who cleared the preliminary examination, and the SBI Clerk Main Result 2025 was declared on June 11 for 14,191 vacancies.
