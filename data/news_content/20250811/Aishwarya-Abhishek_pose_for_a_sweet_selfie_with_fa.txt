Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan are once again giving fans major family goals. Fresh from a holiday with their daughter Aaradhya, the couple was spotted returning to Mumbai. Now, some unseen videos from their vacation, including a heart-warming fan moment, are doing the rounds online and winning hearts.Smiles all around in fan selfieOn Sunday, a fan account posted two videos of the couple posing with a fan. In the first clip, the ‘Jodhaa Akbar’ actress was dressed in an all-black outfit, wearing sunglasses, and casually flipped her hair while posing for the camera. The second showed her leaning on Abhishek to fit into a selfie with the fan at the airport. Both of them smiled warmly for the picture, looking every bit the stylish pair.Abhishek Bachchan hails Aishwarya & Aaradhya|'My daughter does not use social media'Fans can’t stop gushing over themThe couple’s interaction quickly drew love from admirers online. One person commented, “Aishwarya still looks damn pretty.” Another simply wrote, “Cute.” Someone else added, “So beautiful and always kind.” The response proved the couple’s timeless appeal.Family moment on return to MumbaiOn Sunday, video surfaced showing Aishwarya, Abhishek and Aaradhya exiting Mumbai airport after their trip. Aaradhya, looking cheerful, held her mom’s hand as they walked together. The ‘Bunty Aur Babli’ actor walked slightly ahead but stopped to wait and only closed the car door once Aishwarya and Aaradhya were seated. It was a small but thoughtful gesture that fans loved seeing.Abhishek’s recent and upcoming workAbhishek was last seen in the film ‘Kaalidhar Laapata’, also starring Zeeshan Ayub and Daivik Bhagela. His acting won praise from critics and audiences. His next project is Siddharth Anand’s ‘King’, which also stars Shah Rukh Khan, Suhana Khan and Saurabh Shukla in important roles. The film is currently in production.Aishwarya’s last box office hitAishwarya was last seen in Mani Ratnam’s 2023 film ‘Ponniyin Selvan II’.
