The Telangana Council of Higher Education (TGCHE) has officially announced the final phase seat allotment results for Round 3 of the Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical Common Entrance Test (TS EAMCET) 2025. Candidates who participated in the counselling process can now access their seat allotment status online through the official website, tgeapcet.nic.in. This final phase is the last chance for candidates to secure admissions or request changes in their allotted seats before the academic session begins. Students must carefully follow the instructions below to check their results and complete the admission formalities on time.How to check TS EAMCET 2025 final phase seat allotment resultHere’s a simple step-by-step guide to help candidates easily check their seat allotment status online:  Visit the official TS EAMCET counselling website: tgeapcet.nic.in   Click on the link for "Final Phase Round 3 Seat Allotment Result 2025."   Enter your Hall Ticket Number, Registration ID/Login ID, Password, and Date of Birth in the required fields.   Submit the details to view your seat allotment status.   Download and print the allotment letter for future reference.   Direct link to check the TS EAMCET 2025 final phase seat allotment result is available here.Important dates and instructions for candidatesCandidates must be aware of the key deadlines and follow all instructions carefully to confirm their admissions successfully:  Candidates allotted seats in this final phase must pay the seat acceptance fee and complete self-reporting online via the official portal by August 12, 2025.   Those who wish to change their allotted college or branch have the option to physically report at the allotted colleges from August 11 to August 13, 2025.   Colleges will update the final joining details of candidates by August 14, 2025.  About TS EAMCET 2025 TS EAMCET, also known as TG EAPCET, is the state-level entrance examination conducted for admission into undergraduate courses in Engineering, Pharmacy (Medical), and Agriculture across Telangana. The counselling and seat allotment process is conducted based on merit, candidate preferences, and seat availability. Candidates are urged to complete all required formalities within the stipulated deadlines to avoid cancellation of their allotted seats.
