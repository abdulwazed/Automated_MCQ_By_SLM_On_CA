Ananya Panday and Suhana Khan are known for their close friendship and killer style. The duo often makes heads turn whenever they are spotted together. This time, the pair stepped out for a movie night in Mumbai, and they weren’t alone. Suhana’s little brother AbRam joined them, making it a lovely family and friendship outing. A video shows the trio leaving the theatre and heading to their car, sharing smiles and good vibes.Ananya and Suhana shine with AbRam on movie nightAnanya Panday and Suhana Khan have become one of B-Town’s most talked-about friend duos. Their effortless bond and fashion sense always grab attention. Recently, the two were spotted stepping out of a movie theatre with AbRam, Suhana’s adorable younger brother. The three made a casual exit, showing the perfect blend of friendship and family love.Ananya Panday Pauses Shoot to Offer Gratitude at Jaipur’s 'Kale Hanuman Ji' TempleAnanya’s sweet Raksha Bandhan wish for brother AhaanAnanya recently took to Instagram to celebrate Raksha Bandhan with her brother and ‘Saiyaara’ star Ahaan Panday. On August 9, she posted a story featuring a collage of cute pictures of them together. She wrote, “Happy Rakhi Ahaani. Love You.” Suhana’s special message for dad SRK after his national award winIt’s been a proud month for the Khan family as well. Shah Rukh Khan won his first National Award for Best Actor for ‘Jawaan’. Suhana shared a touching post for her father on Instagram, saying, “From bedtime stories to stories that leave a mark, No one tells them like you. Congratulations papa love you the most.” Her words captured the love and admiration she has for her dad’s huge achievement.Suhana and Ananya’s work updatesWhile enjoying these fun moments, both Suhana and Ananya are busy with their careers. Suhana is working on the film ‘King’, where she will share screen space with her father, Shah Rukh Khan. The film is directed by Siddharth Anand and has fans excited. Meanwhile, Ananya was last seen in ‘Kesari Chapter 2’ and is all set to star alongside Kartik Aaryan in ‘Tu Meri Main Tera Main Tera Tu Meri’.
