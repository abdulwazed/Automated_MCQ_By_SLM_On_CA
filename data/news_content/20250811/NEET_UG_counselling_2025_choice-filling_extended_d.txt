NEET UG counselling 2025 choice-filling extended deadline ends today: Check details here
The Medical Counselling Committee has extended the NEET UG 2025 Round 1 choice filling deadline to August 11, 2025, 11:59 PM, offering candidates extra time to lock preferences. While the seat allotment results were scheduled for today, there is no confirmation on a revised release date. Candidates should monitor the MCC website for updates.
