Rohit Sharma's new Lamborghini  NEW DELHI: Indian cricket captain Rohit Sharma has added a brand-new red Lamborghini Urus SE to his garage in Mumbai. The new luxury car sports a personalised number plate — 3015 — a nod to his children’s birthdays and a clever link to his iconic jersey number 45. His earlier car bore the plate 264, representing his record-breaking highest ODI score.The 3015 plate combines ‘30’ for his daughter Samaira’s birthday (December 30) and ‘15’ for his son Ahaan’s birthday (November 15). Its ex-showroom price is reportedly Rs 4.57 crore.On the cricket front, Rohit last played competitively for Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League (IPL).
