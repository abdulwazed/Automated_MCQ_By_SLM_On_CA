During the War 2 pre-release event in Hyderabad, Jr NTR addressed a disruptive fan, emphasizing respect during his speech. He also expressed gratitude to Yash Raj Films and humbly responded to comparisons with Hrithik Roshan, praising Roshan's talent and energy. War 2, directed by Ayan Mukerji, is set for release during Independence Day weekend 2025. At the much-anticipated War 2 pre-release event in Hyderabad, Jr NTR’s Bollywood debut took an unexpected turn when he firmly addressed a disruptive fan during his speech. Amidst thousands of excited fans celebrating the upcoming action-thriller, the RRR star’s sharp warning briefly stole the spotlight. Jr NTR’s firm responseA video shared on X (formerly Twitter) shows Jr NTR saying, “Brother, should I leave? What did I tell you? Stay quiet when I speak. It won’t take me a second to put down the mic and leave the stage.” Thousands of fans had come to celebrate the RRR star’s new project, but the disruption briefly took away the attention.Hrithik and Jr NTR ignite the dance floor in 'War 2' teaser!Comparisons with Hrithik RoshanAt the event, Jr NTR thanked Yash Raj Films for the opportunity and responded to fans comparing him with Hrithik Roshan after the release of the dance number Janaab-e-Aali.Jr NTR said that comparisons between him and Hrithik Roshan can mislead fans, as their work was not a competition but rather complementary. He praised Hrithik as one of the greatest dancers in the country and appreciated the energy Hrithik brought to the set every day. Jr NTR described the 75 days of shooting as a fascinating experience where he learned a lot and recalled how Hrithik’s warm welcome made his Bollywood debut much smoother.About War 2War 2 is an upcoming action-thriller directed by Ayan Mukerji and produced by Yash Raj Films as part of its growing Spy Universe. The film stars Hrithik Roshan, returning as Kabir, alongside Jr NTR, who makes his much-anticipated Bollywood debut.Known for its high-energy stunts and intense drama, War 2 promises a pan-Indian spectacle. It will release in Hindi, Telugu, and Tamil during the Independence Day weekend of 2025. The film features thrilling action scenes, powerful dance numbers like ‘Janaab-e-Aali’, and a showdown between two strong performers.
