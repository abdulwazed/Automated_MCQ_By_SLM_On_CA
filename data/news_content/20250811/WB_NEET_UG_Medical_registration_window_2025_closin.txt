The West Bengal Medical Counselling Committee (WBMCC) has closed the registration window for Round 1 of the NEET UG 2025 counselling will close on Tuesday, August 12, 2025, at 8 PM. Aspiring medical candidates who have not yet registered for the counselling process must complete their applications on the official portal, wbmcc.nic.in, before the deadline.Following the closure of registration, candidates will have until August 13 to complete the mandatory document verification process at their allotted verification centres. This step is crucial for confirming eligibility and finalising participation in the seat allotment process.NEET UG 2025: Schedule and important datesAugust 12, 2025 (8 PM): Final deadline for Round 1 registration and online fee payment.August 13, 2025: Completion of document verification.August 14, 2025 (2 PM): Publication of the list of verified candidates and release of the seat matrix for Round 1.August 14, 2025 (4 PM) to August 17, 2025 (Midnight): Period for candidates to fill and lock their preferred college and course choices online.August 20, 2025 (After 4 PM): Announcement of Round 1 seat allotment results.August 21–23, 2025 (11 AM to 4 PM): Reporting period at allotted colleges for admission confirmation.August 27, 2025: Commencement of Round 2 counselling registration.Candidates must adhere strictly to the timeline to avoid disqualification or loss of opportunity to secure a seat in West Bengal’s medical colleges.WB NEET UG 2025 round 1 counselling: Steps to registerCandidates can follow the steps mentioned here to register for the WB NEET UG counselling 2025:Applicants can follow these streamlined steps to register for the counselling:Visit the official WBMCC website: wbmcc.nic.in.Click the link for NEET UG 2025 Round 1 counselling registration on the homepage.Fill in the required personal and academic details, then submit the form.Log in with the created credentials to complete the application.Upload all necessary documents as per the checklist.Pay the prescribed counselling fee through the online payment gateway.Submit the application and download the confirmation receipt.Retain a printed copy of the confirmation for future reference.Alternatively, candidates can click on the link provided here to apply for the WB NEET UG Counselling 2025.This counselling process is a pivotal step for medical aspirants seeking admission into government and private medical colleges across West Bengal.
