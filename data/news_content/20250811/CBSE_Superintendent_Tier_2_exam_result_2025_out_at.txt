The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has announced the results for the Superintendent Tier 2 examination held on July 5, 2025. This important phase determines which candidates move forward in the recruitment process for the Superintendent post. Candidates who appeared for the exam can now verify their pass/fail status by visiting the official CBSE website at cbse.gov.in. Timely checking of results is crucial as it impacts eligibility for the next stages of selection.  How to check CBSE Superintendent Tier 2 result 2025 Follow these simple steps to quickly check and download your CBSE Superintendent Tier 2 exam result:  Visit the official website: cbse.gov.in   On the homepage, locate the LATEST@CBSE section.   Click on the link titled “Public Notice - CBSE Recruitment Tier - 2 Examination July, 2025 (265 KB) 08/08/2025.”   The result PDF will open on your screen.   Review the document carefully and download it for your records.   It is advisable to print a copy for future reference.Direct link to check the CBSE Superintendent Tier 2 result 2025 is available here.CBSE Superintendent Tier 2 exam: Selection process  The recruitment for the Superintendent post involves multiple stages. It begins with the Tier 1 exam, which is an objective multiple-choice question (MCQ) test serving as a preliminary screening. Candidates who clear Tier 1 advance to the Tier 2 exam, a descriptive written test specifically for the Superintendent position. This is followed by a mandatory Skill Test, which includes a typing assessment relevant to both positions. Finally, shortlisted candidates will undergo Document Verification, where their original certificates and eligibility proofs are scrutinised.
