IBPS PO PET call letter 2025 released at ibps.in  IBPS PO PET call letter 2025: The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has released the IBPS PO PET call letter 2025 for candidates eligible for Pre-Examination Training (PET). The call letter was made available on August 11, 2025, on the official website — ibps.in.Candidates belonging to SC, ST, OBC, PwBD, and minority communities who had opted for PET during registration can now download their call letters using their registration number and password or date of birth. The Pre-Examination Training is scheduled to be conducted from August 11 to August 16, 2025.Pre-exam training available for reserved category candidatesThe PET facility is provided to assist candidates from reserved categories in preparing for the IBPS PO Preliminary Examination 2025. The training covers essential topics, such as reasoning, quantitative aptitude, English language, and time management techniques. It includes mock tests and doubt-clearing sessions to help candidates familiarise themselves with the exam interface.IBPS has also released study materials along with the PET call letter to ensure comprehensive preparation. These resources aim to support eligible candidates ahead of the IBPS PO Preliminary Examination scheduled to be held on August 17, 23, and 24, 2025.Steps to download IBPS PO PET call letter 2025 at ibps.inStep 1: Visit the official IBPS website at www.ibps.in.Step 2: Click on the “CRP PO/MT” section under the “Recruitment” tab.Step 3: Locate and click on the link titled “Download IBPS PO PET Call Letter 2025.”Step 4: Enter your Registration Number and Password or Date of Birth.Step 5: View, download, and print the PET call letter for future reference.Candidates are advised to carry a printed copy of the call letter to the training venue as it is required for entry and verification purposes.Direct link to download the IBPS PO PET call letter 2025Key highlights of IBPS PO PET call letter 2025• Organisation: Institute of Banking Personnel Selection• Exam name: IBPS PO Exam 2025• Post: Probationary Officers• Vacancies: 5,208• Call letter status: Released• PET dates: August 11 to August 16, 2025• Prelims exam dates: August 17, 23, and 24, 2025• Eligible categories: SC, ST, OBC, PwBD, and minority communities• Education qualification: Graduation• Age limit: 20 to 30 years• Selection process: Prelims, Mains, Interview• Application mode: Online• Official website: www.ibps.inBenefits of pre-exam training for eligible candidates• Free support: Provided at no cost to eligible candidates.• Exam interface familiarity: Training on navigating the online exam system.• Mock test access: Practice materials to improve speed and accuracy.• Time management: Strategies to use time effectively in the exam.• Subject-specific training: Focus on reasoning, English, and quantitative aptitude.TOI Education is on WhatsApp now.
