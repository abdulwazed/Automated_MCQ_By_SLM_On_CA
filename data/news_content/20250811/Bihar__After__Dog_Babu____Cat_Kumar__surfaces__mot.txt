NEW DELHI: In a bizarre incident, authorities in Bihar's Rohtas district are investigating how "Cat Kumar," son of "Catty Boss" and "Catiya Devi," applied for a residential certificate. After discovering the shocking applications, district magistrate Udita Singh directed Nasriganj revenue officer Kaushal Patel to look into the matter.
